**Impact:** You're missing 300-400 potential clicks per month from existing impressions.

## 🎓 SEO Best Practices & Tips

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**For Every Page:**
- [ ] Unique, descriptive title tag (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Compelling meta description (150-160 characters)
- [ ] Single H1 tag with target keyword
- [ ] Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Target keyword 2-3 times in content naturally
- [ ] Related keywords throughout (LSI keywords)
- [ ] Internal links to 3-5 related pages
- [ ] External link to 1-2 authoritative sources
- [ ] Optimized images (compressed, alt text)
- [ ] URL is short and descriptive
- [ ] Mobile-friendly design
- [ ] Fast page load speed (<3 seconds)

---

### Content Quality Guidelines

**What Makes Great SEO Content:**

1. **Answer the User's Query Completely**
   - Address search intent directly
   - Provide comprehensive information
   - Go beyond surface-level answers

2. **Use Clear Structure**
   - Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
   - Descriptive headings
   - Bullet points and lists
   - Visual breaks

3. **Write for Humans First, Search Engines Second**
   - Natural, conversational tone
   - Avoid keyword stuffing
   - Focus on being helpful

4. **Include Visual Elements**
   - Images
   - Infographics
   - Tables/charts
   - Videos when relevant

5. **Update Regularly**
   - Keep information current
   - Add new sections as topics evolve
   - Update statistics/data

---

### Local SEO Best Practices

**Boost Local Rankings:**

1. **NAP Consistency** (Name, Address, Phone)
   - Identical across all platforms
   - No variations in formatting
   - Keep updated everywhere

2. **Google Business Profile Optimization**
   - Complete every section
   - Add photos weekly
   - Respond to all reviews
   - Post updates regularly
   - Use relevant categories

3. **Local Citations**
   - Submit to major directories
   - Industry-specific listings
   - Local Austin directories
   - Maintain consistency

4. **Location Pages**
   - Create pages for each service area
   - Include neighborhood names
   - Local landmarks and references
   - Unique content per page

5. **Reviews & Reputation**
   - Actively collect reviews
   - Respond to all (positive & negative)
   - Encourage photo reviews
   - Display reviews on site
