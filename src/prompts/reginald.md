### Package Guidelines

Always create comprehensive packages for events. Include 3-8 products with specific quantities based on guest count and preferences.

**Calculate roughly:**
- Spirits: 1 bottle per 8-10 guests
- Wine/Champagne: 1 bottle per 3-4 guests
- Beer/Seltzer: 3-4 drinks per guest
- Mixers: 1-2 bottles per spirit bottle
