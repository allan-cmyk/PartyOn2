## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book transportation for a large corporate event in Austin?

A: For large corporate events (100+ people), book transportation at least 4-6 months in advance. During peak seasons (SXSW, ACL, F1 weekend), book 6-8 months ahead to ensure availability and best rates.

### Q: What's the most cost-effective transportation option for 200 people in Austin?

A: Charter buses typically offer the best value for groups of 200+, averaging $12-15 per person for a 4-hour event. This includes professional drivers and fuel costs, making it more economical than multiple smaller vehicles.

### Q: How do I coordinate alcohol service with transportation logistics?

A: Partner with a licensed beverage service provider like Party On Delivery who can coordinate with your transportation company. We handle all permits, provide certified bartenders, and ensure safe service while maintaining your event timeline.

### Q: What backup plans should I have for transportation?

A: Always have a dedicated on-call shuttle service and relationships with local rideshare companies. Party On Delivery can help coordinate these services and provide real-time solutions if needed.
