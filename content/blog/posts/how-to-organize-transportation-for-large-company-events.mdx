# How to Organize Transportation for Large Company Events in Austin

Planning transportation for a major corporate event in Austin? Y'all know getting everyone from point A to point B (and maybe C and D) can be trickier than finding parking during SXSW. But don't worry - we've got your back with this comprehensive guide to keeping your team moving smoothly through the ATX.

## Understanding Your Transportation Needs

First things first - you'll need to nail down your headcount and timeline. A successful 300-person corporate event at [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) requires different logistics than a 50-person team outing to [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/). Consider:

- Total number of attendees
- Multiple pickup/dropoff locations
- Event duration and schedule
- Special accommodation needs
- Budget per person

## Timing and Logistics Tips

- Book transportation 2-3 months in advance for large groups
- Add 30-minute buffers between destinations
- Consider Austin traffic patterns (I-35 during rush hour is no joke)
- Plan for weather contingencies (those summer thunderstorms!)
- Coordinate with Party On Delivery for beverage service at your destination

## Budget-Saving Strategies

![Content Image](/images/blog/how-to-organize-transportation-for-large-company-events/how-to-organize-transportation-for-large-company-events-2.webp)


- Book during off-peak seasons (avoid SXSW, ACL, F1 weekends)
- Consider multiple smaller vehicles instead of large buses
- Share transportation with other corporate groups
- Look for package deals with venues and catering services

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book transportation for a large corporate event?

A: For groups of 100+ people, book at least 3 months in advance. During peak seasons (March-May, September-November), consider booking 4-6 months ahead.

### Q: What's the best transportation option for a 200-person corporate event?

A: Usually a combination of 3-4 charter buses (56-passenger) provides the most cost-effective solution. This allows for flexible arrival/departure times and backup capacity.

### Q: Can we serve alcohol on corporate transportation?

A: Yes, but it must be arranged through licensed providers. Party On Delivery can coordinate mobile bar service and ensure all necessary permits are in place.
