There's nothing quite like a Longhorns game day in Austin. The burnt orange sea, the sound of "The Eyes of Texas," and the smell of BBQ smoke filling the air around DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium create an atmosphere that's pure Texas magic. Whether you're a seasoned tailgater or planning your first Austin game day experience, this guide covers everything you need to throw an unforgettable UT tailgate.

## Why UT Tailgating is Special

Austin's tailgate scene combines Texas traditions with the city's unique culture:

- **DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium** seats 100,000+ passionate fans
- **Year-round warm weather** perfect for outdoor celebrations
- **Legendary BBQ** traditions from brisket to sausage
- **Live music** everywhere on game days
- **Texas hospitality** that welcomes all fans
- **Deep traditions** from the Hook 'em Horns to Bevo

## Essential Tailgate Gear

### The Basics

Every successful tailgate needs core equipment:

- **Canopy tent** (10x10 minimum) for Texas sun protection
- **Folding tables and chairs** for food and guests
- **Coolers** with plenty of ice (we recommend one for drinks, one for food)
- **Portable grill** or smoker for authentic Texas BBQ
- **Generator** if you need power for TVs or blenders

### Longhorn Spirit

Show your burnt orange pride with themed gear:

- Longhorn flags and banners
- Burnt orange tablecloths and serving ware
- Bevo mascot decorations
- Hook 'em Horns foam fingers
- UT pennants and yard signs

## Rules and Regulations

### UT Campus Tailgate Rules

Know the rules to avoid issues on game day:

- **Alcohol**: Permitted in designated areas for guests 21+
- **Grills**: Charcoal and propane allowed, no open flames on grass
- **Generators**: Permitted but must be properly ventilated
- **Tents**: Stakes not allowed on pavement, use weights
- **Cleanup**: Pack out everything you bring in

### Parking Lot Policies

Each parking area may have specific rules. Check with lot operators about setup times, prohibited items, and overnight parking restrictions.

## Weather Considerations

Texas weather is unpredictable. Be prepared for:

- **Hot games (Sept-Oct)**: Extra shade, fans, cooler drinks
- **Cold games (Nov-Dec)**: Heaters, warm beverages, layers
- **Rain**: Waterproof canopy sidewalls, covered food storage

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How early should I arrive to get a good tailgate spot?

A: For popular lots near DKR, arrive 4-5 hours before kickoff. Premium spots go fast, especially for big games against SEC rivals. Some dedicated fans arrive the night before for the best locations.

### Q: Can I bring alcohol to a UT tailgate?

A: Yes, alcohol is permitted in designated tailgating areas for guests 21 and over. Glass containers are prohibited in most areas. Keep consumption responsible and designate a driver.

### Q: What's the best way to transport all my tailgate gear?

A: Invest in a quality wagon or cart for hauling gear from parking areas. Collapsible options store easily in your vehicle. Load coolers last so they're first out.

### Q: Can Party On Delivery help with my tailgate?

A: Absolutely! We deliver beer, wine, spirits, and mixers throughout Austin. Stock up before game day and have everything ready to load into your vehicle.

### Q: Are there family-friendly tailgating areas?

A: Yes! The Alumni Center and Longhorn City Limits offer family-friendly environments with activities for kids. Many tailgate areas welcome families, just look for groups with kids present.
