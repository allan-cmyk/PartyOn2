## Navigating the Logistics

Booking a double-decker barge is a big undertaking, but don't worry - we've got your back with some insider tips to make the process smoother:

- Finalise your guest list early. Most barges have strict capacity limits, so you'll want to have an accurate head count before booking.
- Consider transportation. If your guests are coming from all over, you may want to look into options like [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorn-charter.com/) to get everyone to the dock.
- Plan for inclement weather. Many barges have covered areas, but you'll still want to have a backup plan in case of rain or high winds.
- Don't forget the little things. Make sure to coordinate details like parking, boarding procedures, and any special needs or accessibility requirements.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How much do double-decker barges typically cost to rent in Austin?
A: Pricing for double-decker barges in Austin can vary quite a bit depending on the size of the vessel, the package inclusions, and the date/time of your event. As a general guideline, you're looking at anywhere from $1,500 to $5,000 for a 2-4 hour rental, with the higher end being for premium packages on peak dates. Many companies also have hourly rates if you need the barge for a longer period of time.

### Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks on a double-decker barge?
A: This really depends on the specific barge company and their policies. Some may allow you to bring in your own coolers and snacks, while others require you to use their in-house catering and bar services. Be sure to check with the vendor ahead of time to understand any restrictions.

### Q: How far in advance should I book a double-decker barge in Austin?
A: As mentioned earlier, these barges are in very high demand, so it's best to book as far out as possible - especially for weekend dates and popular seasons. The Lone Star Riverboat, for example, recommends booking 6-12 months in advance for weekend events. Some companies may have more last-minute availability on weekdays or during the off-season.

### Q: Are double-decker barges suitable for large groups?
A: Absolutely! The double-decker design allows for ample space to accommodate big groups, with capacities ranging from around 100 to 150 guests depending on the specific barge. Just be sure to have an accurate headcount when booking to ensure you reserve the right size vessel.
