## Pretty in Pink Drinks

No gender reveal is complete without a signature cocktail or two. And when it comes to all things pink, we've got the inside track on Austin's most festive refreshments.

For a playful and totally Instagrammable option, check out the [Frosé from Bufalina](https://www.bufalinapizza.com/) in East Austin. This frozen rosé slushie is the perfect balance of sweet and tart, with a vibrant magenta hue that's sure to wow your guests. 

If you're looking for something a little boozier, the [Watermelon Marg from Guero's Taco Bar](https://www.guerostacobar.com/) on South Congress is a summer staple that works beautifully for gender reveals. Topped with a colorful fruit skewer, this refreshing margarita in shades of pink and red is a real show-stopper.

And for a non-alcoholic option that's still just as festive, the [Pink Lemonade from Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill](https://moonshinegrill.com/) is a total crowd-pleaser. Served with a sugar rim and a playful paper straw, this pink lemonade is the perfect refresher for your reveal party.

## Sweet and Savory Blue Bites

When it comes to the food at your gender reveal, the options for blue-hued treats are truly endless. For a savory twist, we recommend the [Blue Cheese Stuffed Dates from Fricano's Deli](https://fricanodeli.com/) in downtown Austin. These bite-sized morsels are a sophisticated play on the classic bacon-wrapped date, with a tangy blue cheese filling that's sure to delight your guests.

If you're looking for something a little sweeter, the [Blue Velvet Cupcakes from Opa! Bakery](https://opabakery.com/) in the Warehouse District are an absolute showstopper. Topped with a fluffy, azure-tinted frosting and a dusting of edible glitter, these indulgent cupcakes are almost too pretty to eat (almost).

And for a fun, interactive option, you can't go wrong with [Blue Cotton Candy from Barton Creek Farmers Market](https://www.bartoncreekfarmersmarket.org/). Set up a DIY cotton candy station and let your guests get creative, spinning their own personal tufts of vibrant blue sugar.

## Thoughtful Presentation

Of course, the key to any successful gender reveal is making sure your snacks and drinks look as good as they taste. Here are a few of our top tips for creating an Instagram-worthy spread:

• Incorporate creative, gender-reveal-themed serving vessels, like blue or pink mason jars, test tubes, or champagne flutes.

• Use colorful, themed garnishes like pink and blue sprinkles, edible glitter, or fruit skewers in contrasting hues.

• Consider hiring a local calligrapher to create custom place cards or signage for your refreshment stations.

• Set up a "reveal" station where guests can assemble their own custom drinks or treats, complete with a dramatic reveal moment.

• Don't forget the little details, like pink and blue paper straws, napkins, or even colored ice cubes to really drive the theme home.

### Q: What's the best way to incorporate the reveal moment into the party?

A: There are so many fun ways to build anticipation and excitement around the reveal! Some popular options include having guests guess the gender and then dramatically unveiling the results, or setting up a "reveal station" where guests can assemble their own customized treats and then discover the big news. You can also incorporate creative gender reveal elements like confetti cannons, balloon pops, or even a special cake-cutting moment to make the reveal truly unforgettable.

### Q: How can I make sure my gender reveal party is environmentally friendly?

A: Sustainability is important, especially for a celebration as joyful as a gender reveal! Consider using compostable or reusable serveware, offering non-alcoholic options like the pink lemonade from Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill, and working with caterers and vendors who prioritize eco-friendly practices. You can also encourage guests to carpool or use local transportation like [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorncharterbus.com/) to reduce your event's carbon footprint.

### Q: What's the average budget for a gender reveal party in Austin?

A: The cost of a gender reveal party in Austin can vary quite a bit depending on the size, location, and level of customization. As a general guideline, you can expect to spend anywhere from $50 to $150 per guest for a more elevated, full-service event. This would typically include venue rental, catering, alcohol, and any additional decor or entertainment. Of course, you can also opt for a more DIY approach and keep things closer to $25 to $75 per guest. The key is to work with your budget and prioritize the elements that are most important to you and your guests.
