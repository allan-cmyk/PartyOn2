## Choosing the Venue

![Hero Image](/images/blog/how-to-plan-a-cake-cutting-gender-reveal/how-to-plan-a-cake-cutting-gender-reveal-0.webp)


The foundation of any epic gender reveal is the venue, and Austin is brimming with incredible options. For an intimate gathering, consider a private room at a trendy spot like [Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill](https://moonshinegrill.com/) or [Scholz Garten](https://www.scholzgarten.com/). 

Looking to go all out? The [Brazos Hall](https://brazos-hall.com/) event space is a stunning urban loft with exposed brick and high ceilings, perfect for a swanky soirée. Or, if you want to soak up some of that famous Austin sunshine, the [Hotel Indigo Austin Downtown](https://www.ihg.com/hotelindigo/hotels/us/en/austin/ausdt/hoteldetail) has a gorgeous rooftop terrace that would be ideal for a cake-cutting celebration.

## Picking the Perfect Cake

No gender reveal is complete without the iconic cake-cutting moment, so you'll want to choose a design that's sure to wow your guests. Reach out to local Austin bakers like [Sugar Mama's Bakeshop](https://www.sugarmamasbakeshop.com/) or [Polkadots Cupcake Factory](https://www.polkadotscupcakefactory.com/) and collaborate on a custom cake that captures the spirit of your event.

From classic blue or pink ombre creations to cakes with fun gender-themed toppers, the options are endless. And don't forget to coordinate the cake's color scheme with your overall event decor - you want everything to look cohesive and Instagram-worthy!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: When is the best time to host a gender reveal party?
A: The optimal time to host a gender reveal party is typically between 16-20 weeks of pregnancy. This allows enough time for the gender to be determined, but still leaves plenty of time before the baby's arrival to enjoy the celebration.

### Q: Do we have to do a cake-cutting reveal?
A: Not at all! While a cake-cutting reveal is a classic gender reveal tradition, there are plenty of other creative ways to unveil the baby's gender, like popping a confetti-filled balloon or having guests guess and then revealing the color of a special treat.

### Q: How much should we budget for a gender reveal party?
A: Gender reveal parties can range widely in cost, depending on the size, location, and level of customization. As a general guideline, you can expect to spend anywhere from $1,000 to $5,000 for a celebration with 50-100 guests. Factors like catering, decor, and entertainment will all impact the final budget.
