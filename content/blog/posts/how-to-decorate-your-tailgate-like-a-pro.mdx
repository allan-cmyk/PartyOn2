# How to Decorate Your Tailgate Like a Pro

It's that time of year again, Longhorns fans - football season is here! Whether you're gearing up for a [UT home game at DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium](https://texaslonghorns.com/facilities/dkr-texas-memorial-stadium/1) or joining the party at one of Austin's legendary tailgating spots, it's time to kick your pregame festivities into high gear. 

As an Austin-based premium alcohol delivery service specializing in weddings, bachelor/bachelorette parties, boat parties, and corporate events, the team at [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/) knows a thing or two about setting the scene and elevating any celebration. We're here to share our top tips for decorating your tailgate like a pro, so you can take your gameday experience to the next level.

## Embrace the Longhorns Spirit

No tailgate is complete without a heavy dose of Longhorns pride. Start by decking out your space in the iconic burnt orange and white. Hang a giant [UT flag](https://www.bookstorebadger.com/c-1372-texas-longhorns-flags.aspx) or banner, and fill your coolers with [Longhorns koozies](https://www.bookstorebadger.com/c-1305-texas-longhorns-koozies.aspx) to keep your drinks cold in style.

For a personalized touch, create custom cornhole boards or yard games featuring the [Longhorns logo](https://brand.utexas.edu/resources/logos/). You can even go all out with inflatable Bevo decorations or a [UT-themed tent](https://www.universitytent.com/texas-longhorns-tents/) to create an unforgettable gameday atmosphere.

## Incorporate Local Flair

![Hero Image](/images/blog/how-to-decorate-your-tailgate-like-a-pro/how-to-decorate-your-tailgate-like-a-pro-0.webp)


As an Austin-based company, we love celebrating the unique spirit of our city. Incorporate local touches that give your tailgate a distinctly "Keep Austin Weird" vibe. 

Hang [string lights](https://www.homedepot.com/p/Philips-30-Light-Indoor-Outdoor-String-Light-Set-with-Clear-Bulbs-366577/206959274) and colorful flags or banners from local [East Side King](https://www.eastsidekingaustin.com/) or [Veracruz All Natural](https://www.veracruzallnatural.com/) food trucks. Set up a [Lone Star Beer](https://lonestarbeer.com/) pong table or display a [Dale's Pale Ale](https://www.oskarblues.com/our-beer/dales-pale-ale) keg tower. 

Don't forget to incorporate nods to iconic Austin landmarks like the [Texas State Capitol building](https://tspb.texas.gov/prop/tc/tc/capitol.html), the [Congress Avenue Bridge](https://www.austintexas.org/listings/ann-richards-congress-avenue-bridge-bats/2841/), or the [Texas Longhorn herd](https://tpwd.texas.gov/state-parks/barton-creek-greenbelt/texas-longhorn-herd) that roams Pease Park.

## Create a Cozy Lounge Area

While the action may be on the field, your tailgate should be a comfortable oasis for you and your crew. Set the scene with plush outdoor furniture, cozy [Aztec-patterned blankets](https://www.westelm.com/products/aztec-jacquard-throw-t4603/?catalogId=71&sku=3295984&cm_ven=PLA&cm_cat=Google&cm_pla=Bedding%20%3E%20Throws%20%26%20Blankets&region_id=477400&cm_ite=3295984_14522969137&gclid=CjwKCAjw6raYBhB7EiwABge5Khw76Cp_5oHAhRrHXHCxD7_Iq0cQDcW-oN6CyoUvyAHo7pYr4JihmhoCOLIQAvD_BwE), and plenty of pillows. 

For a touch of luxury, rent a [Brazos Hall](https://brazos-hall.com/) tent or canopy to shield your lounge area from the sun. Add some string lights and a portable speaker to really set the mood.
