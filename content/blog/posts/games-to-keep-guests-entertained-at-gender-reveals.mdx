## Gender Reveal Trivia

![Hero Image](/images/blog/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals-0.webp)


What better way to get the party started than with a little friendly competition? Set up a gender reveal-themed trivia game and watch the laughs (and friendly rivalries) unfold. ## Guess the Baby Food
This classic party game is a surefire hit at any gender reveal. Fill jars with different baby food flavors and have your guests take a guess. You can even add a little twist by having them taste test the flavors blindfolded for extra laughs.

If you really want to up the ante, offer a prize for the person who gets the most correct. Maybe a free gift basket from our gender reveal package or a gift card to [Franklin Barbecue](https://franklinbbq.com/) - the ultimate Austin food destination.

## Guess the Candy in the Jar

![Content Image](/images/blog/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals-1.webp)

Similar to the baby food game, this one challenges your guests to estimate the number of candies in a jar. You can go with classic gender reveal colors like pink and blue, or mix it up with a variety of sweets.

This is a great activity to have running throughout the party, allowing people to come back and re-guess as the night goes on. The person with the closest estimate at the end takes home the jar of sugary goodness!

## Onesie Decorating Contest
Get creative with this one! Set up a station with plain white onesies, fabric markers, glitter, and other decorative supplies. Challenge your guests to create the most unique, gender-themed onesie design.

You can even turn this into a friendly competition, with prizes for the most creative, funniest, or most heartfelt onesie. Display the masterpieces throughout the party and let your guests vote on the winners.

## Guess the Due Date

![Content Image](/images/blog/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals/games-to-keep-guests-entertained-at-gender-reveals-2.webp)

This one's a classic! Have your guests write down their guesses for the baby's due date on slips of paper, then collect them and draw a winner at the end of the party. The person who comes closest to the actual due date takes home a special prize.

You can even make it more interesting by having everyone chip in a few bucks to create a small cash pot for the winner. Just be sure to keep track of all the guesses and have the big reveal be a surprise!
