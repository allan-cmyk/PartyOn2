## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need a boating license to rent a party boat?
A: Most rental companies provide a licensed captain, but if you plan to operate the boat yourself, you'll need a Texas Boating License. This can be obtained online through the Texas Parks & Wildlife Department.

### Q: What's the best time of year for a Lake Travis bachelor party?
A: Late spring (April-May) and early fall (September-October) offer the best combination of warm weather and smaller crowds. Summer months are popular but expect peak pricing and busy waterways.

### Q: How early should I book a party boat rental?
A: For bachelor parties, especially during peak season, book at least 2-3 months in advance. Holiday weekends may require 4-6 months advance booking.
