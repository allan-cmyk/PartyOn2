## Why Lake Travis is Bachelor Party Paradise

Lake Travis isn't just any body of water – it's 65 miles of pure party potential with some of the clearest water in Texas. Just 20 miles northwest of downtown Austin, this aquatic playground offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. With countless coves for anchoring, party-friendly beaches, and waterfront restaurants, it's no wonder Lake Travis has become the go-to destination for bachelor celebrations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What's the best time of year for a Lake Travis bachelor party?
A: Late spring (April-May) and early fall (September-October) offer ideal weather conditions with temperatures in the 75-85°F range and less crowded waterways. Summer months are popular but expect higher prices and bigger crowds.

### Q: Do I need a boating license to rent?
A: In Texas, if you were born on or after September 1, 1993, you need a boating license. However, most rental companies provide licensed captains or can arrange for certification courses.

### Q: What about alcohol on boats?
A: While alcohol is permitted on boats in Lake Travis, the captain must remain sober. Party On Delivery can arrange pre-stocked beverages and setup, ensuring everything is legal and safe.

### Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: For weekend rentals during peak season (May-September), book 3-6 months in advance. Off-season might require only 1-2 months' notice, but earlier is always better.
