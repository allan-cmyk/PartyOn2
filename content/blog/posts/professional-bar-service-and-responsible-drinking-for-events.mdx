## Responsible Drinking Best Practices

![Content Image](/images/blog/professional-bar-service-and-responsible-drinking-for-events/professional-bar-service-and-responsible-drinking-for-events-1.webp)


Keep your event classy and safe with these proven strategies:

- Schedule food service from popular spots like [Salt Lick BBQ](https://saltlickbbq.com/) during drinking hours
- Offer water stations and non-alcoholic alternatives
- Partner with rideshare services or [Austin Charter Service](https://austincharterservice.com/) for safe transportation
- Consider timing - end bar service an hour before event conclusion

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How many bartenders do I need for my event?
A: Generally, plan for one bartender per 75 guests for beer/wine service, or one per 50 guests for full bar service. For corporate events, we recommend additional staff to reduce wait times during peak periods.

### Q: What's included in professional bar service?
A: Professional packages typically include TABC-certified bartenders, complete bar setup, ice, mixers, garnishes, and all necessary supplies. Insurance coverage and proper permits are also included.

### Q: How can I ensure responsible drinking at my corporate event?
A: Implement drink ticket systems, provide substantial food options, arrange transportation services, and work with professional bartenders trained in monitoring alcohol consumption.
