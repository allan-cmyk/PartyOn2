## Frequently Asked Questions

![Content Image](/images/blog/professional-bar-service-and-responsible-drinking-for-events/professional-bar-service-and-responsible-drinking-for-events-2.webp)


### Q: How many bartenders do I need for my event?
A: Generally, plan for 1 bartender per 75 guests for beer/wine service, or 1 per 50 guests for full bar service. Large corporate events may require additional staff for optimal service flow.

### Q: What licenses are required for alcohol service in Austin?
A: All bartenders must have current TABC certification. Venues may require additional permits and insurance coverage, which Party On Delivery can assist with.

### Q: Can we bring our own alcohol for the bar service?
A: Yes, many Austin venues allow client-provided alcohol. However, it must be served by TABC-certified professionals. Party On Delivery can help coordinate purchasing and delivery.

### Q: What's included in professional bar service setup?
A: Standard setups include ice, mixers, garnishes, tools, and glassware. Premium packages may include specialty cocktail stations, champagne towers, or custom bar designs.
