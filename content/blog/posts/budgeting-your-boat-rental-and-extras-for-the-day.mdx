## Calculating Additional Costs

Once you've nailed down the boat rental itself, it's time to start thinking about all the extras that'll make your day on the water truly unforgettable. From food and drinks to music and activities, there are lots of ways to customize your boat party experience.

### Food and Drinks

No boat party is complete without an abundance of refreshments! You'll need to account for the cost of any catering, snacks, or beverages you plan to have onboard. Some boat rental companies offer in-house food and drink packages, while others allow you to bring your own. 

If you go the DIY route, be sure to budget for things like:
* Coolers and ice
* Bottled water, soda, and juices
* Beer, wine, and cocktail mixers
* Plastic cups, napkins, and utensils
* Any specialty food items or catering

### Music and Entertainment

No boat party is complete without a killer playlist and some fun on-the-water activities. Make sure to factor in the cost of any speakers, sound systems, or live music you want to have. You'll also need to budget for any inflatable toys, water sports equipment, or party games you plan to bring aboard.

### Transportation and Parking

Depending on the size of your group and where your boat is docked, you may need to arrange for transportation to and from the marina. Services like [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorn-charter.com/) can make getting everyone to the boat a breeze. And don't forget to account for any parking fees at the launch site.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book my boat rental?
A: It's best to book your boat rental as early as possible, especially during peak seasons like summer. Many popular rental companies can fill up months in advance, so try to lock in your reservation at least 3-6 months prior to your event.

### Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks on the boat?
A: Most boat rental companies allow you to bring your own food and non-alcoholic beverages on board. However, many have restrictions on outside alcohol, so be sure to check with the rental provider ahead of time. [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/) can simplify this process by handling all your boat party alcohol needs.

### Q: Do I need a special license or permit to operate the boat?
A: For most standard boat rentals in the Austin area, you won't need a specific boating license. The rental company will provide a captain or guide to operate the vessel. However, if you're renting a larger yacht or planning to operate the boat yourself, you may need to provide proof of a valid boating license.

### Q: What should I bring with me on the boat?
A: In addition to your food, drinks, and music, it's a good idea to pack things like sunscreen, hats, towels, and a change of clothes. You may also want to bring any water sports equipment, games, or other activities you plan to enjoy on the water.
