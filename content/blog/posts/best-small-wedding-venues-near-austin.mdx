## Best Small Wedding Venues Near Austin

When you think "Austin wedding", you probably envision sprawling ranch estates, trendy downtown lofts, or towering rooftop venues. But the capital city also has an abundance of charming, intimate wedding spaces perfect for those seeking a more personal, cozy celebration. From historic manors to hidden garden oases, let's explore some of the best small wedding venues near Austin.

## The Wildflower Barn

![Hero Image](/images/hero/wedding-hero-garden.webp)


Nestled just 30 minutes from downtown Austin in Dripping Springs, [The Wildflower Barn](https://www.thewildflowerbarn.com/) offers a naturally beautiful setting for your special day. This 3,000-square-foot barn is surrounded by rolling hills, wildflower fields, and a serene pond - creating a dreamy, rustic-chic ambiance. The venue can accommodate up to 200 guests and includes a bridal suite, groom's lounge, and a commercial catering kitchen. Packages start at $6,500.

## Ma Maison

For an elegant, French-inspired affair, consider [Ma Maison](https://mamaison.com/) in Dripping Springs. This 8,000-square-foot venue features a limestone chapel, private bridal suite, and gorgeous gardens. Capacity is 300 guests, and packages range from $8,500 to $18,500. Ma Maison also offers in-house catering, bar service, and coordination - making it a full-service wedding destination.

## The Addison Grove

Located just 20 minutes southwest of downtown Austin, [The Addison Grove](https://theaddisongrove.com/) is a beautiful, 10-acre property with a restored 1940s barn, lush gardens, and a charming farmhouse. The indoor-outdoor space can accommodate 200 guests, and packages start at $6,000. This family-owned venue is a true hidden gem, perfect for intimate, rustic-chic celebrations.

## Pecan Springs Ranch

Nestled on 20 acres in the heart of the Hill Country, [Pecan Springs Ranch](https://www.pecanspringsranch.com/) offers a serene, natural setting for your wedding. The venue features a sprawling lawn, courtyard, and a historic white-washed barn that can host up to 250 guests. Packages start at $6,000, and in-house catering, bar, and coordination services are available.

## The Allan House

For a classic, elegant wedding, consider the [The Allan House](https://www.theallanhouse.com/) in downtown Austin. This 19th-century Victorian mansion can accommodate 150 guests and includes a private courtyard, wrap-around porch, and bridal suite. Venue rental starts at $5,000 on weekdays and $7,000 on weekends. The Allan House's historic charm and central location make it a sought-after intimate wedding destination.

## The Greenhouse

Nestled on the banks of Onion Creek, [The Greenhouse](https://www.onionccreekwinery.com/the-greenhouse) at Onion Creek Winery is a serene, botanical-inspired venue perfect for small, eco-conscious weddings. The 2,000-square-foot greenhouse space can host up to 120 guests, and packages start at $5,000. Couples can also opt for an outdoor ceremony on the lawn or terrace. In-house catering, bar, and coordination are available.

## Practical Tips for Booking a Small Wedding Venue

When searching for the perfect intimate wedding space, here are a few helpful tips:

- **Prioritize your guest list**: Determine your ideal headcount first, then search for venues that can comfortably accommodate your group.
- **Consider off-peak days**: Weekday or Sunday weddings often have more availability and lower pricing at small venues.
- **Book early**: Intimate wedding spots book up fast, especially during peak seasons. Secure your date at least 12-18 months in advance.
- **Inquire about minimums**: Some small venues require a minimum number of guests or minimum spend. Make sure the numbers work for your budget.
- **Ask about all-inclusive packages**: Many small wedding spots offer all-inclusive options that bundle the venue, catering, bar, and coordination - simplifying your planning.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What is the average cost of a small wedding venue in Austin?

A: The average starting price for small wedding venues in the Austin area is around $5,000 to $8,000. Pricing can vary widely depending on the venue's size, location, amenities, and services included. Many of the most popular intimate spaces have starting rates in the $6,000 to $10,000 range.

### Q: How far in advance should I book a small wedding venue?

A: It's recommended to book your small wedding venue at least 12-18 months in advance, especially if you're planning to tie the knot during peak wedding season (March-October). The most sought-after intimate spaces can book up 2 years or more in advance. Be prepared to be flexible on your date if you have your heart set on a particular venue.

### Q: Do small wedding venues offer in-house catering and bar service?

A: Many of the top small wedding venues in Austin do offer in-house catering and bar service as part of their all-inclusive packages. This can simplify your planning and ensure a cohesive, high-quality experience for your guests. However, some venues may allow you to bring in your own licensed, insured caterer and bartender if you prefer. Be sure to ask about the catering and bar policies when touring potential venues.
