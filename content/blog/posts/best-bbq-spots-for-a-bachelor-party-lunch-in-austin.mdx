# Best BBQ Spots for a Bachelor Party Lunch in Austin

Planning a bachelor party in Austin? You can't skip the legendary Texas BBQ scene. As local experts in bachelor party planning, we've rounded up the absolute best spots to treat your crew to mouth-watering brisket, ribs, and all the fixings that make Austin BBQ world-famous. From iconic institutions to hidden gems, here's your guide to the perfect bachelor party BBQ lunch experience.

## Top BBQ Joints for Large Groups

### Franklin Barbecue
[Franklin Barbecue](https://franklinbbq.com/)
900 E 11th St, Austin, TX 78702

The undisputed king of Austin BBQ, Franklin's is worth the wait for special occasions. Pro tip: For bachelor parties of 8+ people, you can pre-order with their catering service to skip the infamous line. Their melt-in-your-mouth brisket is literally worth planning your entire day around.

### La Barbecue
[La Barbecue](https://www.labarbecue.com/)
2401 E Cesar Chavez St, Austin, TX 78702

Known for their perfectly crusty brisket and spicy house-made sausage, La Barbecue offers pre-orders for large groups. The outdoor seating area is perfect for rowdy bachelor parties, and they're BYOB-friendly (hint: Party On Delivery can stock you up).

### LeRoy & Lewis Barbecue
[LeRoy & Lewis Barbecue](https://www.leroyandlewis.com/)
121 Pickle Rd, Austin, TX 78704

For something different, LeRoy & Lewis serves up "new school BBQ" with unique cuts and creative sides. Their food truck setup at Cosmic Coffee makes it perfect for a casual bachelor party lunch with craft beer options on-site.

## Hidden Gem: Micklethwait Craft Meats
[Micklethwait Craft Meats](http://craftmeatsaustin.com/)
1309 Rosewood Ave, Austin, TX 78702

This food trailer serves some of Austin's best craft BBQ without the tourist crowds. Their jalapeño cheese grits and homemade moon pies are legendary. Perfect for smaller bachelor parties (6-12 people) looking for an authentic Austin experience.

## Upscale BBQ Experience: Interstellar BBQ

![Content Image](/images/blog/best-bbq-spots-for-a-bachelor-party-lunch-in-austin/best-bbq-spots-for-a-bachelor-party-lunch-in-austin-1.webp)

[Interstellar BBQ](https://www.interstellarbbq.com/)
12233 Ranch Rd 620 N, Austin, TX 78750

For a more refined bachelor party lunch, Interstellar BBQ offers an elevated take on Texas classics. Their brisket au poivre and peach tea-brined turkey are mind-blowing. They have indoor seating and take reservations for large groups.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What's the best time to visit Austin BBQ joints for a bachelor party?
A: For most spots, aim for 11am-1pm to ensure the best selection. Some places sell out of popular items by mid-afternoon. Weekdays typically have shorter lines than weekends.

### Q: How much BBQ should I order per person?
A: Plan for about 1/2 pound of meat per person, plus 2-3 sides. Most places offer combo plates that work perfectly for groups.
