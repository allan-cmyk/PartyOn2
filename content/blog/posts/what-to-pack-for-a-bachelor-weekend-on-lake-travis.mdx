## What to Pack for a Bachelor Weekend on Lake Travis

Austin is the perfect destination for an unforgettable bachelor party, and there's no better place to celebrate than on the sparkling waters of Lake Travis. As the premier alcohol delivery service for weddings, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and corporate events in Austin, we know a thing or two about packing for an epic Lake Travis getaway.

Whether you're planning a day on a party boat, an evening of bar-hopping on the shore, or a weekend of watersports and bonding with the guys, we've got you covered with this essential packing list for your Lake Travis bachelor party.

## The Essentials

![Hero Image](/images/hero/bach-hero-party-bus.webp)


First and foremost, pack plenty of sun protection. With Texas' year-round sunny skies, you'll want to lather on the SPF, especially if you'll be spending the day out on the water. Bring a wide-brimmed hat, high-quality polarized sunglasses, and a lightweight, quick-drying rashguard or cover-up.

Comfortable, water-friendly footwear is a must. [Chaco](https://www.chacos.com/) or [Teva](https://www.teva.com/) sandals are perfect for moving seamlessly from boat to shore. And don't forget a pair of sneakers or boat shoes for any land-based activities.

Of course, you'll need to stay hydrated. Pack a reusable water bottle and plenty of electrolyte-replenishing drinks like Gatorade or Powerade. And don't forget the sunscreen - you'll be glad you packed the extra bottle when you're out on the lake all day.

## Party Essentials

No bachelor party is complete without a killer playlist and some festive party supplies. Load up your phone or portable speaker with your group's favorite tunes, and don't forget to pack some portable phone chargers to keep the music flowing.

For decorations and group activities, hit up your local party supply store (or [shop online with Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/bach-parties)) for inflatable pool floats, glow sticks, beach balls, and any other fun, Instagram-worthy props you can dream up.

And speaking of Instagram - be sure to pack a waterproof phone case or [GoPro](https://gopro.com/) to capture all the epic moments out on the water.

## Swim and Sports Gear

If you're planning to spend a significant amount of time in the water, be sure to pack your swimsuits, towels, and any water sports equipment you'll need. Popular Lake Travis activities include wakeboarding, tubing, paddleboarding, and jet skiing, so check with your boat rental company or Airbnb host about what gear they provide.

Don't forget to bring a change of clothes for any post-water activities. A lightweight, quick-drying outfit will keep you comfortable as you transition from lake to land.

## Grooming and Recovery Essentials

After a long day out on the water, you'll want to freshen up before heading out for the evening festivities. Pack your usual toiletries, but don't forget some key recovery items like ibuprofen, antacids, and hydrating face masks.

And speaking of evening plans, be sure to pack a nice outfit or two for any dressy dinners or nightclub visits you have planned. [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) and [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) are two popular spots to dress up for.
