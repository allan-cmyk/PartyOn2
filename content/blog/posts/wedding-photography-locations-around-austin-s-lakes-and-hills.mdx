When it comes to planning your dream wedding, the city's picturesque lakes, rolling hills, and unique venues offer a wealth of breathtaking photo opportunities. ## Scenic Lakeside Venues

![Hero Image](/images/hero/wedding-hero-garden.webp)


[The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) is a stunning hilltop event space overlooking the serene waters of Lake Travis. With its panoramic views, cascading waterfalls, and lush gardens, this venue provides the perfect backdrop for your wedding photos. Just a short drive from downtown Austin, the Oasis offers a secluded, serene setting for your special day.

![The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/The-Oasis-Aerial-View.jpg)

Another popular lakeside destination is [Lakeway Resort and Spa](https://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/auslw-lakeway-resort-and-spa/), a luxury resort with a private beach and sprawling lawns overlooking Lake Travis. Couples can capture romantic portraits against the backdrop of the lake, or gather their wedding party for fun, candid shots on the resort's jetty or docks.

## Hilltop Havens

For couples seeking a more elevated setting, Austin's picturesque hilltops offer stunning panoramic views of the city and surrounding countryside. [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/), a luxurious hotel located in the heart of downtown, features a rooftop pool and event space with sweeping vistas of the Austin skyline. Imagine saying "I do" against the backdrop of the city's gleaming high-rises and the distant Hill Country.

![Fairmont Austin Rooftop](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/wp-content/uploads/sites/74/2020/07/Fairmont-Austin-Rooftop-Pool.jpg)

Just outside the city, [Arnosky Family Farms](https://arnoskyfamilyfarms.com/) provides a serene, rural setting for your wedding photos. This picturesque hilltop venue boasts rolling meadows, a historic barn, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Capture timeless portraits of your wedding party framed by the stunning Texas Hill Country.

## Urban Oases

For a more modern, urban aesthetic, consider venues located within the heart of downtown Austin. [Brazos Hall](https://brazoshall.com/) is a stunning event space housed in a renovated warehouse, offering a mix of industrial chic and contemporary elegance. Couples can take advantage of the venue's exposed brick walls, large windows, and unique architectural details to create truly memorable wedding photos.

![Brazos Hall](https://brazoshall.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/Brazos-Hall-Entryway.jpg)

Alternatively, the [Four Seasons Hotel Austin](https://www.fourseasons.com/austin/) provides a luxurious, sophisticated setting for your wedding day. With its manicured grounds, elegant ballrooms, and sweeping views of Lady Bird Lake, this premier hotel is a favorite among discerning couples seeking a truly upscale experience.

## Practical Tips for Wedding Photography Locations

When selecting your wedding photography locations, consider the following tips:

• Prioritize scenic backdrops that complement your wedding style and vision.
• Visit potential venues in person to get a feel for the space and lighting.
• Coordinate with your wedding photographer to ensure they can capture your desired shots.
• Discuss logistics like parking, accessibility, and any venue restrictions.
• Plan for backup locations in case of inclement weather.
