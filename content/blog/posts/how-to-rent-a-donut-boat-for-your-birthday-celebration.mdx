## How to Rent a Donut Boat for Your Birthday Celebration

Austin is known for its vibrant party scene, and there's no better way to celebrate a birthday than on the water. One of the most unique and fun options for your next birthday bash? Renting a donut boat on Lake Travis.

Donut boats are the latest craze in Austin's party boat scene, and they offer a truly one-of-a-kind experience for your birthday celebration. Imagine cruising around the lake, sipping cocktails, and soaking up the sun with your closest friends - all while floating on a giant, donut-shaped vessel.

If you're looking to make your birthday unforgettable, a donut boat rental is the way to go. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to plan the perfect donut boat birthday party on Lake Travis.

## What is a Donut Boat?

![Hero Image](/images/blog/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration-0.webp)


A donut boat is exactly what it sounds like - a large, circular boat shaped like a giant donut. These unique vessels can accommodate up to 12 people and are the perfect party platform for birthdays, bachelorette parties, corporate events, and more.

Donut boats are equipped with a variety of features to make your celebration a blast. Most include built-in coolers, Bluetooth speakers, and even slides for easy water access. Some even have shade canopies to keep you cool on hot Texas days.

## Where Can You Rent a Donut Boat in Austin?

The epicenter of donut boat rentals in Austin is [Lake Travis](https://www.visitaustin.org/things-to-do/lakes-rivers/lake-travis/), the stunning, 65-mile-long reservoir just outside the city. Several local companies offer donut boat rentals, with pick-up and drop-off locations at various marinas around the lake.

Some of the top donut boat rental providers in the Austin area include:

- [Donut Boat ATX](https://loveandboat.com/donut-boats/) - Located at Emerald Point Marina on Lake Travis
- [Laketravis.com](https://www.laketravis.com/) - Offers donut boat rentals at multiple Travis County locations
- [Oasis Marinas](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) - Rents donut boats out of their marina at The Oasis on Lake Travis

Prices for donut boat rentals typically start around $300-$500 for a 2-4 hour rental, depending on the size of the boat and the day/time of your event.

## How to Plan a Donut Boat Birthday Party

![Content Image](/images/blog/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration-1.webp)


Ready to make waves for your birthday? Here are the key steps to planning an unforgettable donut boat celebration:

### 1. Choose Your Rental Company
Research the top donut boat providers in the Austin area and compare options like boat size, rental duration, and pricing. Be sure to read reviews and ask about any additional fees or restrictions.

### 2. Pick a Date and Time
Donut boats are in high demand, especially during the busy summer months. Book your rental as far in advance as possible, keeping in mind that weekends and holidays will be the most competitive.

### 3. Decide on Guest List and Headcount
Donut boats typically accommodate 8-12 people, so make sure you have an accurate headcount before reserving your boat. You'll also want to factor in any food, drinks, or additional equipment you plan to bring.

### 4. Plan Your Birthday Itinerary
Beyond just renting the donut boat, you'll want to plan out how you'll spend your time on the water. Will you cruise the lake, anchor and swim, or play music and dance? Make sure to coordinate with your rental company on any special requests or add-ons.

### 5. Gather Essentials and Party Supplies
Don't forget to pack everything you'll need for a day on the lake - swimsuits, towels, sunscreen, speakers, coolers, and any decorations or party favors. [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/) can even handle the alcohol and mixers so you don't have to worry about stocking the boat.

### 6. Arrange for Transportation
Most donut boat rentals require you to pick up and drop off the boat from the marina. Make sure you have a plan for getting your whole group to and from the lake, whether that's renting a [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorncharterbus.com/) or coordinating personal vehicles.

## Insider Tips for a Memorable Donut Boat Birthday

From personal experience, here are some of our top tips for making the most of your donut boat birthday celebration:

• Bring plenty of sunscreen and water-friendly accessories like polarized sunglasses. The Texas sun can be brutal, even on the water.

• Pack a portable speaker and make a killer party playlist to keep the energy high all day.

• Coordinate with your rental company on any special add-ons or catering options. Many can provide everything from snacks and drinks to on-board grills and watersports equipment.

• Plan for some non-water activities too, like docking at a lakeside restaurant like [The Oasis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) for a birthday dinner.

• Designate a sober driver or utilize a [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/) driver service to ensure everyone gets home safely.

## Frequently Asked Questions

![Content Image](/images/blog/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration/how-to-rent-a-donut-boat-for-your-birthday-celebration-2.webp)


### Q: How much do donut boat rentals typically cost in Austin?
A: Donut boat rental rates in the Austin area generally range from $300-$600 for a 2-4 hour rental, depending on the size of the boat and the rental company. Prices are usually quoted per boat, not per person.

### Q: Do I need a boating license to rent a donut boat?
A: No, you do not need a boating license to rent and operate a donut boat in Texas. The rental company will provide all necessary safety equipment and instructions.

### Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks on the donut boat?
A: Yes, most donut boat rental companies allow you to bring your own snacks, beverages, and even small grills or coolers. Just be sure to clean up after your party and dispose of any trash properly.

### Q: How many people can fit on a donut boat?
A: Donut boats in Austin typically accommodate between 8-12 passengers, depending on the specific model. Be sure to provide an accurate headcount when booking your rental.

### Q: Are there any age or experience requirements to rent a donut boat?
A: Donut boat rentals generally have a minimum age requirement of 21+ to operate the vessel. The rental staff will provide a safety orientation, but no prior boating experience is necessary.
