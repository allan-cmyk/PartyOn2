## Food and Drink Ideas for Your BYOB Austin Boat Party

Ahh, the classic Austin boat party - a beloved tradition that's equal parts sun-soaked fun and local flavor. As an Austin-based premium alcohol delivery service, we at Party On Delivery have seen (and helped plan) our fair share of these aquatic adventures. 

So whether you're setting sail on the tranquil waters of Lake Travis or cruising along Lady Bird Lake, we're here to share our top food and drink ideas to make your BYOB boat party the talk of the town.

## Stick to Texas Classics

![Hero Image](/images/hero/lake-travis-yacht-sunset.webp)

When it comes to boat party eats, you can't go wrong with some good ol' Lone Star State staples. Think juicy [Franklin Barbecue](https://franklinbbq.com/) brisket sliders, spicy [Hatch chili](https://www.texashatch.com/) queso dip, and crisp [Tito's Handmade Vodka](https://www.titosvodka.com/) cocktails. 

For a true Austin twist, you could even curate a "Texas Tapas" spread with miniature chicken-fried steaks, bite-sized tacos, and petite kolaches. Just be sure to stock up on plenty of [Big Red soda](https://www.bigred.com/) and icy Shiner Bock to keep your guests hydrated under the hot sun.

## Get Creative with Charcuterie
No boat party is complete without a towering charcuterie board overflowing with local flavors. Start with a selection of cured meats from spots like [Salt & Time](https://saltandtime.com/) or [Dai Due](https://daiduebutcher.com/), then add in artisanal Texas cheeses, colorful fruit, and homemade jams.

For a playful twist, consider a "build-your-own" charcuterie bar where guests can customize their own mini boards. Just provide an array of tasty toppings and let the creativity flow. Don't forget the crackers, crusty bread, and [Topo Chico](https://www.topochicousa.com/) to wash it all down!

## Elevate Your BYOB Game
As the name implies, BYOB boat parties allow you to curate your own signature beverage selection. But why settle for basic beer and wine when you can elevate your drink menu with premium cocktail mixers and garnishes?

Consider a DIY bloody mary bar stocked with [Tito's Handmade Vodka](https://www.titosvodka.com/), spicy [Yellowbird hot sauces](https://yellowbirdsauce.com/), and all the fixings. Or create a summery spritz station with [Topo Chico](https://www.topochicousa.com/), seasonal fruit, and a selection of artisanal [Treaty Oak Distilling](https://treatyoakdistilling.com/) spirits.

And don't forget the ice! Keep your drinks chilled with large blocks or spheres from a spot like [Austin Ice Company](https://austinicecompany.com/). Trust us, your guests will thank you.

## Bite-Sized Desserts
After a day of indulging in savory eats and refreshing cocktails, your crew will surely have room for something sweet. Mini desserts like bite-sized cupcakes, petite pies, and adorable cake pops are perfect for boat party snacking.

For an extra Texas twist, consider [Sugar Mama's Bakeshop](https://sugarmamabakeshop.com/) for their famous "Texas sheet cake bites" or [Tiny Pies](https://tinypies.com/) for their adorable hand-held pies. Just be sure to have plenty of napkins on hand!

## Practical Party Planning Tips
Of course, no Austin boat party is complete without a few insider tips to keep things running smoothly. Here are a few of our top recommendations:

- Rent a cooler or two to keep your food and drinks properly chilled throughout the day. [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorn-charter.com/) offers cooler rental packages perfect for boat parties.
- Designate a "bartender" to manage the drink station and ensure your guests don't overindulge (nothing ruins a party faster than an unsafe captain!).
- Pack plenty of snacks and non-alcoholic options like water, soda, and juices to keep everyone hydrated.
- Bring along easy-to-eat finger foods that don't require utensils or plates.
- Make a playlist of upbeat tunes to set the mood, but be mindful of volume levels to avoid disturbing nearby boats.
- Scope out the best spots on the lake ahead of time, like the coves near [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) or the calm waters of Lady Bird Lake.
