## What is a Party Barge?

### Party Barge Features

These boats are designed for entertaining:

- **Large flat deck**: Open space for socializing
- **Multiple seating areas**: Conversation zones
- **Shade coverage**: Canopy or bimini tops
- **Built-in coolers**: Keep drinks accessible
- **Bluetooth audio**: Connect your playlist
- **Swim ladder**: Easy water access
- **BYOB friendly**: Most allow alcohol

## Choosing the Right Size

### Guest Count Guidelines

Book based on actual attendees:

- **15-20 guests**: Standard party barge
- **20-30 guests**: Large party barge or double-decker
- **30-40 guests**: Premium double-decker
- **40+ guests**: Consider two boats or private charter

### Space Considerations

Think about activities:

- Dancing requires more space
- Food/drink station needs
- Shade for sun-sensitive guests
- Water entry/exit flow

### What's Included

Standard rental includes:

- Boat and safety equipment
- Fuel for typical usage
- Basic orientation
- Life jackets
- Often: coolers, Bluetooth

Usually extra:

- Captain (some boats require)
- Water toys
- Extra fuel
- Extended hours

## Booking Tips

### When to Book

Timing matters:

- **Summer weekends**: 4-6 weeks ahead
- **Holiday weekends**: 2-3 months ahead
- **Fall/Spring weekends**: 2-3 weeks ahead
- **Weekdays**: Often last-minute available

### Questions Before Booking

Ask every rental company:

1. What's the total cost including fees?
2. What's your cancellation policy?
3. Is the boat BYOB friendly?
4. What's the captain situation?
5. What amenities are included?
6. Where can we go on the lake?
7. What happens if weather is bad?

## Amenities to Look For

### Must-Haves

Essential features:

- Adequate shade coverage
- Working Bluetooth system
- Clean restroom (if available)
- Swim ladder
- Sufficient seating
- Ice chests/coolers

### Nice-to-Haves

Premium features:

- Water slide
- Upper deck (double-decker)
- Lily pad or swim platform
- Premium sound system
- TV screens
- Grill (some boats)

## Safety Considerations

### Capacity Limits

Never exceed posted capacity:

- It's illegal and dangerous
- Insurance won't cover incidents
- Rental company can refuse departure
- Coast Guard can cite you

### Designate Responsibility

For large groups:

- Sober captain or pay for one
- Person in charge of headcount
- First aid point person
- Someone monitoring weather

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I rent multiple party barges for a very large group?

A: Yes, many companies accommodate this. You can coordinate boat rentals to stay together on the lake. Some even offer group discounts.

### Q: Is a captain required for party barges?

A: It depends on the size and company policy. Larger double-deckers often require a captain. Smaller barges may just need an orientation.

### Q: How do I split costs fairly for a group boat rental?

A: Divide total cost by number of guests, or have organizers cover the boat and guests bring their own drinks. Party On Delivery can help you calculate beverage costs per person.

### Q: What if some people cancel after booking?

A: Most deposits are non-refundable. Collect money upfront and set a cutoff date for cancellations to protect the organizer.
