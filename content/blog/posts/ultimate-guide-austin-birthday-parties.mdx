## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book a birthday venue in Austin?

A: For restaurants, book 2-4 weeks ahead for private rooms. Popular venues and boat rentals need 4-6 weeks during peak season. Major milestone birthdays (30th, 40th, 50th) warrant earlier planning.

### Q: What's the best area for a birthday bar crawl in Austin?

A: Rainey Street offers walkable bungalow bars perfect for groups. East 6th has a hip, diverse scene. West 6th caters to upscale tastes. 6th Street itself is classic Austin party energy.

### Q: Can I bring my own cake to Austin restaurants?

A: Most restaurants allow outside cakes with advance notice. Some charge a cake-cutting fee ($2-5 per person). Always confirm when booking.

### Q: What's the best time of year for an outdoor birthday party in Austin?

A: March-May and September-November offer the best weather. Avoid July-August unless you have pool access or indoor backup plans. Winter months are mild but unpredictable.
