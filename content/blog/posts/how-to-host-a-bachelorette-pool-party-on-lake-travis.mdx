## Plan the Perfect Bachelorette Itinerary

With the venue and bar setup sorted, it's time to plan the rest of your bachelorette party itinerary. Here are some of our top recommendations for making the most of your Lake Travis pool party:

- Start the day with a relaxing brunch at a local Austin hotspot like [Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill](https://www.moonshinepatio.com/) or [Veracruz All Natural](https://veracruzmexican.com/). Fuel up before a day of fun in the sun.

- Rent a boat or pontoon from a lake outfitter like [Texas Boat World](https://texasboatworld.com/) and spend the afternoon cruising around the lake. Don't forget your swimsuits!

- Book a private cabana or lounge area at your chosen venue to ensure the whole crew has a comfortable spot to hang out.

- Hire a photographer to capture all the bachelorette party memories. [Kimberly Harrell Photography](https://www.kimberlyhphotography.com/) and [Becca Lea Photography](https://beccaleaphotography.com/) are two local Austin-based options.

- Schedule some fun group activities like water sports, volleyball, or even a bachelorette-themed pool party game. The [Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) has all sorts of on-site activities.

- Leave ample time for relaxing poolside, sipping cocktails, and soaking up the gorgeous Hill Country views.

## Prioritize Safety and Logistics

When hosting a pool party on Lake Travis, safety and logistics are key. Here are a few practical tips to keep in mind:

- Arrange reliable transportation to and from the venue, whether that's a chartered bus, rideshare, or designated drivers. [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorn-charter.com/) is a great local option.

- Make sure everyone has a way to get home safely at the end of the night, whether that's a hotel room, ride share, or sober driver.

- Designate a few sober, responsible party planners to oversee the logistics and ensure everyone has a good time.

- Pack plenty of sunscreen, hats, and other sun protection essentials. The Texas sun can be brutal, even on the water.

- Stay hydrated by interspersing alcoholic drinks with plenty of water and non-alcoholic options.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book a Lake Travis venue for a bachelorette party?

A: It's best to book your Lake Travis venue at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak season like summer weekends. Many popular spots like the [Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) and [Lakeway Resort and Spa](https://www.lakewayresortandspa.com/) fill up quickly, so the earlier you can secure your date, the better.

### Q: Do I need to hire any additional staff or vendors for a bachelorette pool party?

A: It depends on the size and scope of your event, but you may want to consider hiring some additional staff or vendors to help things run smoothly. This could include bartenders, servers, security, a photographer, DJ or band, and any other specialists you may need. Party On Delivery can provide professional bartenders and a full bar setup to take the hassle out of the drinking situation.

### Q: What should I include in a bachelorette pool party welcome bag?

A: Some fun ideas for a bachelorette pool party welcome bag include:
- Personalized koozies, sunglasses, or other party favors
- Sunscreen, lip balm, and other sun protection essentials
- Snacks like chips, candy, and hydrating fruit
- Hangover remedies like pain relievers and electrolyte drinks
- Any other festive or practical items the bride-to-be will appreciate

### Q: How can I make my bachelorette pool party more unique or memorable?

A: There are so many ways to put a personal spin on your Lake Travis bachelorette bash! Some ideas include hiring an entertainer like a male revue or dance troupe, setting up a glam photo booth, hosting a pool Olympics with fun party games, or surprising the bride with a special performance or activity. Get creative and focus on what the guest of honor will love most.
