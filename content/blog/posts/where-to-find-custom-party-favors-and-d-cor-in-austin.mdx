## Where to Find Custom Party Favors and Décor in Austin

If you're planning an unforgettable party in Austin, whether it's a bachelorette bash, corporate shindig, or boat bash on Lake Travis, the right custom party favors and decor can take your event to the next level. As an Austin-based premium alcohol delivery service, we've seen it all when it comes to party planning - and we know the local hot spots for sourcing unique, personalized touches.

From handmade wearables to chic tablescape accessories, Austin is brimming with talented local artisans and event stylists who can make your vision a reality. Whether you're looking for custom koozies, handpainted glassware, or eye-catching backdrops, here are some of our favorite spots to find one-of-a-kind party supplies in the Live Music Capital.

## Bachelorette Party Favors and Accessories

![Hero Image](/images/hero/bach-hero-rainey.webp)


When it comes to Austin bachelorette parties, you can't go wrong with accessories and favors that capture the city's vibrant, bohemian spirit. [Sunroom ATX](https://www.sunroomdecor.com/) in East Austin is a must-visit for gorgeous, Instagram-worthy decorations and apparel. Their selection of custom neon signs, LED light-up props, and handpainted items like tumblers and totes are perfect for photo ops.

For something a little more elevated, check out [Dear Delilah](https://deardelilahshop.com/) in the South Congress district. This charming boutique offers an array of luxe bachelorette essentials, from satin robes and silky hair scrunchies to bedazzled sunglasses and custom koozies. The owners can also create custom welcome boxes packed with goodies for the bride-to-be and her crew.

## Unique Tablescape Decor

One of our favorite parts of planning Austin events is sourcing the perfect tablescape accessories. [Loot Vintage Rentals](https://www.lootrentals.com/) in East Austin has an incredible collection of vintage and antique furniture, glassware, and serving pieces that can instantly elevate any party spread. From mismatched china and crystal chandeliers to mid-century modern lounge chairs, their showroom is a treasure trove of design inspiration.

For a more whimsical, bohemian look, [The Beehive](https://www.thebeehiveatx.com/) in South Austin has an amazing selection of colorful, handcrafted table linens, placemats, and napkin rings. Their in-house event stylists can also create custom tablescapes and floral arrangements to match your party theme.

## Custom Party Signage and Backdrops

For eye-catching, Instagram-worthy backdrops and custom party signage, [Milk + Honey Studio](https://milkandhoneystudio.com/) in East Austin is a real hidden gem. They specialize in handpainted backdrops, neon signs, and laser-cut acrylic installations that can transform any space. Their team can also create custom pieces to match your party theme, from tropical palm leaves to glitzy disco balls.

Another great option is [Pen and Petal](https://penandpetal.com/) in South Austin, which offers a wide range of custom calligraphy and signage services. Whether you need handlettered welcome signs, table numbers, or oversized dance floor decals, their talented artists will bring your vision to life.

## Personalized Party Favors

One of the best ways to make your Austin event feel extra special is with custom party favors that guests can take home. [Steel City Pops](https://www.steelcitypops.com/) is known for their delicious, all-natural popsicles, but they also offer a fun custom popsicle program. You can have your logo, monogram, or even a cute design printed directly on the popsicle sticks.

For edible favors with a personal touch, [Sugar Mama's Bakeshop](https://www.sugarmamasbakeshop.com/) in South Austin creates custom cookies, cakes, and other sweet treats that are almost too beautiful to eat. They can incorporate your party theme, colors, or even photos into the design.

## Where to Find Stylish Austin Event Rentals

Of course, no party is complete without the right furniture, lighting, and other rentals to set the mood. [Loot Vintage Rentals](https://www.lootrentals.com/) has an amazing selection, as we mentioned before, but you can also check out [Premiere Events](https://www.premiereeventrentals.com/) for a wider range of modern, high-end pieces.

Their showroom in North Austin is filled with stylish lounge furniture, tabletop accessories, and even bars and drink stations that are perfect for corporate events, weddings, and other celebrations. The team at Premiere Events can also provide planning and design services to help pull your vision together.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where can I find custom koozies and other personalized party favors in Austin?
A: For unique, custom party favors like koozies, tumblers, and apparel, check out Sunroom ATX in East Austin and Dear Delilah in the South Congress district. Both boutiques offer a great selection of handpainted, monogrammed, and customized accessories perfect for bachelorette parties, weddings, and other Austin celebrations.

A: Some of the most popular Austin venues for bachelorette parties include the Fairmont Austin, The Oasis on Lake Travis, and Brazos Hall.
