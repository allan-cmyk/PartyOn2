## Frequently Asked Questions

![Content Image](/images/blog/best-live-entertainment-ideas-for-austin-corporate-functions/best-live-entertainment-ideas-for-austin-corporate-functions-2.webp)


### Q: How far in advance should I book entertainment for a corporate event in Austin?
A: Book main entertainment acts at least 3-4 months in advance, especially during peak seasons (March-May and September-November). Popular local bands and premium venues often book 6+ months out for corporate events.

### Q: What's the typical budget range for corporate event entertainment in Austin?
A: Most corporate events in Austin spend between $2,500-$7,500 on entertainment, including sound equipment and setup. This can vary significantly based on the type of entertainment and duration of the event.

### Q: Can you combine different types of entertainment throughout the event?
A: Yes, and it's often recommended! For example, start with ambient music during check-in, transition to interactive entertainment during networking, and end with a high-energy band. [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com) can help coordinate timing and transitions.

### Q: Do I need to provide food and drinks for the entertainment staff?
A: Yes, it's standard practice to provide meals for entertainment staff working events longer than 4 hours. This should be factored into your catering headcount.
