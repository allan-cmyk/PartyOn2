# Best Live Entertainment Ideas for Austin Corporate Functions

Planning a corporate event in Austin? Y'all are in luck! As the Live Music Capital of the World, Austin offers endless entertainment options to make your business function memorable. From intimate acoustic sets to full-blown production shows, let's explore the best live entertainment ideas that'll have your colleagues talking about your event for months to come.

## Live Music Acts That Strike the Right Chord

Austin's music scene is legendary, and incorporating local talent into your corporate event adds authentic flavor. Here are some top options:

* Jazz ensembles from [Selmer's Music](https://selmersmusic.com/) - Perfect for cocktail hours
* Acoustic singer-songwriters from [Continental Club](https://continentalclub.com/) - Great for networking events
* Cover bands from [C3 Presents](https://www.c3presents.com/) - Ideal for larger gatherings

For upscale corporate events, consider booking through [Austin Classical Guitar](https://www.austinclassicalguitar.org/) for sophisticated background music that won't overpower conversations.

## Venue-Entertainment Pairings

Choose the right venue to complement your entertainment:

[Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) - Their Congressional Ballroom provides excellent acoustics for live bands, with state-of-the-art sound systems included.

[The Sunset Room](https://www.sunsetroomaustin.com/) - Perfect for intimate jazz performances with its art deco ambiance and downtown views.

[Brazos Hall](https://brazoshall.com/) - Ideal for larger productions with its warehouse-style space and built-in stage.

## Frequently Asked Questions

A: Most corporate events in Austin allocate $1,500-5,000 for entertainment. This can vary significantly based on the type of performance and duration of the event.

### Q: How early should I book entertainment for a corporate event?
A: Book main entertainment acts at least 3-4 months in advance, especially during peak seasons (March-May and September-November) in Austin.
