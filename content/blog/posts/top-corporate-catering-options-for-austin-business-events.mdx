# Top Corporate Catering Options for Austin Business Events

Planning a corporate event in Austin? Y'all know food can make or break any business gathering, whether it's a quarterly meeting, client appreciation dinner, or company-wide celebration. Let's dive into the best corporate catering options that'll keep your team talking (in a good way) long after the last bite.

## Premium Downtown Venues with In-House Catering

The [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) stands out as one of downtown's most prestigious corporate event venues, offering world-class in-house catering services. Their culinary team specializes in everything from breakfast buffets to multi-course plated dinners, with special attention to dietary restrictions and local ingredients.

For a more intimate setting, [The Allan House](https://theallanhouse.com/) provides a historic mansion backdrop with approved catering partners who understand the unique demands of business events.

## Local Restaurant Catering Services

[Franklin Barbecue](https://franklinbbq.com/) offers corporate catering that'll impress out-of-town clients with authentic Texas BBQ. Pro tip: Book at least 2 months in advance for large groups.

[Easy Tiger](https://easytigerusa.com/) provides excellent breakfast and lunch options, featuring their famous artisan breads and pastries. Perfect for morning meetings or casual lunch presentations.

## Planning Tips for Success

![Content Image](/images/blog/top-corporate-catering-options-for-austin-business-events/top-corporate-catering-options-for-austin-business-events-2.webp)


* Book catering 6-8 weeks in advance for large corporate events
* Plan for 1.5 servings per person for buffet-style service
* Consider dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free options)
* Account for 2-3 drinks per person for 2-hour evening events
* Request staff for setup, service, and cleanup

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What is the typical cost per person for corporate catering in Austin?
A: Corporate catering in Austin typically ranges from $25-100 per person, depending on the service style and menu selection. Breakfast meetings average $25-35 per person, while plated dinners can range from $65-100 per person.

### Q: How far in advance should I book corporate catering?
A: Book corporate catering at least 6-8 weeks in advance for large events (50+ guests) and 2-4 weeks for smaller gatherings. Premium dates and popular venues may require even longer lead times.

### Q: Do I need to provide anything for the caterers?
A: Most full-service caterers provide all necessary equipment, but verify what's included. Some venues require specific setup requirements or have restrictions on certain caterers.
