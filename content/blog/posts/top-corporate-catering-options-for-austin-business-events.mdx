## Budget Planning Tips

When planning your corporate catering budget, consider these typical cost breakdowns:

• Food: 40-50% of total budget
• Beverages: 20-30% of total budget
• Staff & Service: 15-20% of total budget
• Equipment & Rentals: 10-15% of total budget

Pro Tip: Many caterers offer significant discounts for morning or early afternoon events compared to evening service.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What is the typical lead time needed for corporate catering in Austin?
A: Most corporate caterers require a minimum of 2-3 weeks' notice for standard events. For large events (200+ guests) or during peak seasons (spring and fall), book 2-3 months in advance to secure your preferred caterer and ensure adequate staffing.

### Q: How much should I budget per person for corporate catering in Austin?
A: Budget $25-45 per person for basic buffet service, $45-85 for plated meals, and $15-35 for food truck service. Add $15-25 per person for basic bar service or $25-40 for premium bar packages. These ranges include staff and basic equipment.

### Q: Can I mix multiple catering vendors for one event?
A: Yes, many Austin events successfully combine multiple vendors (e.g., food trucks plus bartending service). However, ensure each vendor has proper insurance and check venue policies regarding multiple vendors. Party On Delivery regularly coordinates with food vendors to provide seamless service.
