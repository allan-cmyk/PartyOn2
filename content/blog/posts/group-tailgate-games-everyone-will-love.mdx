![Hero Image](/images/blog/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love-0.webp)


No tailgate is complete without a heated round (or five) of cornhole. This classic lawn game is easy to learn, endlessly entertaining, and perfect for groups of all sizes. ## Beer Pong

For the more competitive (and thirsty) tailgaters, beer pong is a classic choice. Set up a table, fill some cups, and get ready for an epic showdown. The [Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) has plenty of outdoor space perfect for a raucous game, or you can keep it simple in the parking lot of your favorite Austin dive bar like [Javelina](https://www.javelinaaustin.com/).

Pro tip: Stock up on solo cups, ping pong balls, and a sturdy folding table from [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/) so you can easily transport and set up your beer pong arena anywhere. And don't forget the most crucial ingredient - a steady supply of your favorite Austin beers!

## Giant Jenga

![Content Image](/images/blog/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love-1.webp)


For a game that's equal parts skill and spectacle, you can't beat giant Jenga. Watch in delight (or horror) as your friends carefully extract blocks, trying not to topple the towering stack. This oversized version of the classic game is perfect for large groups and makes for some amazing photo ops.

The best places to set up a giant Jenga tournament are wide-open event spaces like [Brazos Hall](https://brazos-hall.com/) or the grassy knolls around [Darrell K Royal-Texas Memorial Stadium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darrell_K_Royal%E2%80%93Texas_Memorial_Stadium). Just make sure to have your [Party On Delivery tailgating package](https://partyondelivery.com/) on hand with all the beverages to keep the thirsty players hydrated.

## Ladder Golf

If you're looking for a game that's easy to learn but still challenging, ladder golf is a fantastic choice. Also known as "bolo toss," this lawn game involves throwing a pair of golf ball-attached strings, trying to wrap them around the rungs of a small ladder-like structure. It's a great way to get everyone involved and build up some friendly competition.

For the ultimate ladder golf experience, head to the [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) and set up a tournament on their sprawling event lawns. Or pack a portable ladder golf set and play right in the heart of the action at [Scholz Garten](https://www.scholzgarten.com/) - just make sure to grab a [Party On Delivery tailgating package](https://partyondelivery.com/) to fuel the fun.

## Yard Pong

![Content Image](/images/blog/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love/group-tailgate-games-everyone-will-love-2.webp)


If you're looking to switch up the classic beer pong formula, yard pong is a fantastic alternative. Similar to cornhole, this game involves tossing ping pong balls into a series of cups placed on the ground. It's a bit more challenging than cornhole, making for an extra competitive and entertaining experience.

For the best yard pong setup, head to an expansive event space like [Brazos Hall](https://brazos-hall.com/) or the grassy fields around [DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darrell_K_Royal%E2%80%93Texas_Memorial_Stadium).
