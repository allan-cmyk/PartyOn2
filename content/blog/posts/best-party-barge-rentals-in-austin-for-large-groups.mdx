[The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) is a popular destination for Lake Travis boat parties, and they offer several party barge options to choose from. ## Tips for Renting a Party Barge in Austin

- **Book early**: Party barge rentals, especially on peak weekends, can fill up quickly. We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for the best selection and pricing.

- **Consider the size**: Make sure to choose a barge that can comfortably accommodate your group. Most barges max out at 12-14 people, so plan accordingly.

- **Factor in extras**: In addition to the hourly rental rate, you'll want to budget for things like fuel, damage deposits, and potential add-ons like catering or watersports equipment.

- **Designate a captain**: Many party barge companies require you to have a licensed captain to operate the vessel. If no one in your group has the proper credentials, the rental company can usually provide one for an additional fee.

- **Pack the essentials**: Don't forget to bring plenty of sunscreen, towels, music, and of course, your favorite drinks and snacks. Most barges have coolers and basic amenities, but it's always good to come prepared.

- **Time it right**: Aim to book your party barge for the hottest, sunniest part of the day. The mornings and evenings can get a bit chilly on the water.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I choose the best party barge for my event?
A: When selecting a party barge, consider the size of your group, the amenities you want (slide, wet bar, etc.), and your overall budget. It's also a good idea to read reviews and ask about any hidden fees or restrictions. The companies listed above are all reputable options with great party barge rentals in Austin.

### Q: Can I bring my own alcohol on a party barge?
A: Most party barge rental companies in Austin allow you to bring your own alcohol, but there may be restrictions on glass containers or cans. To make things easy, you can also look into alcohol delivery services like [Party On Delivery](https://partyondelivery.com/boat-parties) that can provide coolers, ice, cups, and premium beverages right to your boat.

### Q: How long should I rent a party barge for?
A: The standard rental period for party barges in Austin is 3-4 hours, which gives you plenty of time to cruise the lake, swim, and party. However, some companies offer hourly rates or full-day packages if you want to extend your time on the water.

### Q: Do I need a boating license to operate a party barge?
A: In most cases, no - the party barge rental company will either provide a licensed captain to operate the boat or allow someone in your group to drive it if they have the proper credentials. Just be sure to check the rental policies ahead of time.
