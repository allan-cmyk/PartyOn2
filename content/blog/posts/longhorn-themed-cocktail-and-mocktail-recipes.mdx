## Burnt Orange Margarita

![Hero Image](/images/blog/longhorn-themed-cocktail-and-mocktail-recipes/longhorn-themed-cocktail-and-mocktail-recipes-0.webp)


What could be more fitting for a Longhorns tailgate than a classic margarita with a burnt orange twist? This vibrant cocktail is perfect for sipping pre-game as you gather with friends at iconic Austin spots like [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) or [Brazos Hall](https://brazos-hall.com/).

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz tequila
- 1 oz triple sec
- 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
- 1 oz orange juice
- 1 tbsp burnt sugar simple syrup

To make the burnt sugar simple syrup, caramelize 1 cup of sugar in a skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly until it reaches a deep amber color. Carefully add 1 cup of water and stir to dissolve the caramelized sugar. Let cool before using.

Rim a rocks glass with a lime wedge and dip in coarse salt. Add ice, then pour in the tequila, triple sec, lime juice, orange juice, and burnt sugar simple syrup. Stir to combine and garnish with a lime wedge.

## Longhorn Lemonade Mocktail

For those looking to keep it alcohol-free, our Longhorn Lemonade mocktail is a refreshing and festive option. Serve these up in souvenir cups for the kids (or the designated drivers) at your tailgate, or mix up a pitcher to share.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 cup burnt sugar simple syrup
- 4 cups sparkling water
- Lemon slices for garnish

In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice and burnt sugar simple syrup. Top with the sparkling water and stir to combine. Serve over ice and garnish each glass with a lemon slice.

## Burnt Orange Punch

![Content Image](/images/blog/longhorn-themed-cocktail-and-mocktail-recipes/longhorn-themed-cocktail-and-mocktail-recipes-1.webp)


This vibrant, citrusy punch is a crowd-pleasing option for your Longhorns tailgate. Make a big batch ahead of time and keep it chilled in a [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorncharterbus.com/) cooler to serve throughout the day.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups freshly squeezed orange juice
- 2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 cup burnt sugar simple syrup
- 2 liters ginger ale
- Orange and lemon slices for garnish

In a large punch bowl or beverage dispenser, combine the orange juice, lemon juice, and burnt sugar simple syrup. Just before serving, add the ginger ale and stir gently to combine. Garnish with orange and lemon slices.

## Presentation is Key

When it comes to tailgating, the right glassware and garnishes can really take your cocktails and mocktails to the next level. Consider serving your Longhorn-themed drinks in souvenir cups or mason jars for a rustic, Texas-inspired vibe. Rim the glasses with sugar or salt, and don't forget to garnish with fresh citrus slices, mint sprigs, or even a toasted marshmallow for an extra touch of fun.
