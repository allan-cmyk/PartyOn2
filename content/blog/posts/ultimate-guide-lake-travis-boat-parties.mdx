## The Ultimate Guide to Lake Travis Boat Parties

Lake Travis is Austin's playground, and a boat party on its sparkling waters is a quintessential Texas experience. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, bachelor/bachelorette weekend, or just gathering friends for an epic day, this guide covers everything you need for the perfect lake day.

At Party On Delivery, we help hundreds of boat parties stock up every season. This comprehensive guide shares everything we've learned about making lake days unforgettable.

## Why Lake Travis?

Lake Travis offers the perfect setting for boat parties:

- **65+ miles of shoreline** with countless coves and beaches
- **Crystal clear water** perfect for swimming
- **Waterfront restaurants and bars** for docking and dining
- **Boat rental options** for every group size and budget
- **Year-round boating** (though spring and fall are ideal)
- **BYOB friendly** culture throughout the lake

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need a boating license to rent on Lake Travis?

A: Texas requires a boater education course for anyone born on or after September 1, 1993 who operates a boat with 15+ horsepower. Many rental companies provide captains, which solves this requirement.

### Q: Can I bring glass bottles on a boat rental?

A: Most rental companies prohibit glass for safety reasons. Stick to cans and plastic containers. Party On Delivery can help you find canned alternatives for your favorite drinks.

### Q: How early should I book a boat for a weekend party?

A: For peak season weekends (Memorial Day through Labor Day), book 4-8 weeks in advance. Popular double-decker barges book up even earlier. Weekday rentals have more last-minute availability.

### Q: What happens if it rains?

A: Most rental companies have weather policies. Light rain usually means you can still go out (it can actually be refreshing). Severe weather or lightning means rescheduling. Check cancellation policies when booking.

### Q: Can Party On Delivery deliver to the marina?

A: Yes! We deliver to many Lake Travis marinas and boat ramps. Coordinate delivery timing with your rental pickup, and your drinks will be cold and ready.
