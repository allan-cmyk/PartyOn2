# Corporate Holiday Party Ideas for Austin Companies

Planning a company holiday celebration in Austin? Y'all are in for a treat! As the live music capital of the world and home to some of the most unique venues in Texas, Austin offers endless possibilities for creating memorable corporate festivities. Let's dive into some creative ideas that'll have your team talking well into the new year.

## Stunning Downtown Venues with Skyline Views

Nothing says "Austin celebration" quite like our gorgeous downtown skyline. The [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/) offers sophisticated spaces with floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing downtown views. For a more industrial-chic vibe, [Brazos Hall](https://brazoshall.com/) features exposed brick walls and a stunning rooftop deck perfect for cocktail hours.

Want something truly unique? Consider [The Contemporary Austin-Jones Center](https://thecontemporaryaustin.org/jones-center/), where your team can celebrate surrounded by world-class art installations.

## Lake Travis Sunset Soirées

![Hero Image](/images/blog/corporate-holiday-party-ideas-for-austin-companies/corporate-holiday-party-ideas-for-austin-companies-0.webp)


Take advantage of Austin's natural beauty with a lakeside celebration. [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/) is famous for its sunset views and multi-level party spaces. For a more intimate gathering, [County Line on the Lake](https://countyline.com/) combines Texas BBQ with stunning water views.

## Food Truck Festivities

Keep it Austin-weird with a food truck party! The [Thicket Food Park](https://www.thicketaustin.com/) offers a perfect outdoor setting with string lights and picnic tables. Pro tip: Party On Delivery can set up mobile bars alongside the food trucks for a full-service experience.

Popular food truck combinations include:
- Tacos + craft cocktails
- BBQ + local craft beer
- Asian fusion + sake stations
- Dessert trucks + coffee/espresso bars

Keep your team safe with dedicated transportation. [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorn-charter.com/) offers everything from luxury shuttles to party buses. For smaller groups, consider partnering with rideshare services or arranging designated drivers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book a corporate holiday party in Austin?
A: Book your venue 4-6 months in advance for December events. Popular dates like the first two weekends of December fill up quickly. Some premium venues may require booking up to a year ahead.

### Q: What's the average budget for a corporate holiday party in Austin?
A: Most Austin companies spend between $100-200 per person for holiday parties, including venue, food, beverages, and entertainment. Higher-end events at luxury venues can reach $250+ per person.

### Q: Can Party On Delivery handle last-minute corporate events?
A: Yes! While advance notice is preferred, we specialize in quick-turnaround service for corporate events. Contact us at least 48 hours before your event, and we'll work to accommodate your needs.

### Q: What are the most popular dates for corporate holiday parties in Austin?
A: The first two weekends of December are most popular, followed by mid-week dates in early December. Consider Thursday evenings for better venue availability and potentially lower rates.
