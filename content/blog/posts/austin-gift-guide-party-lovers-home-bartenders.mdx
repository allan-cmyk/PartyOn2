## Austin Gift Guide: Best Gifts for Party Lovers & Home Bartenders

Austin is a city that knows how to celebrate. From backyard BBQs and rooftop happy hours to Lake Travis boat parties and epic bachelorette weekends, entertaining is woven into the fabric of this city. If you've got someone on your gift list who loves to host, mix drinks, or throw unforgettable gatherings, you've come to the right place.

As Austin's premier alcohol delivery service, we've helped stock thousands of parties, events, and celebrations across the city. We know what makes a great home bar, which tools every host needs, and how to elevate any gathering from ordinary to extraordinary.

This gift guide covers every budget - from small stocking stuffers to full bar setups that will make any entertainer's heart sing.

## Stocking Stuffers & Small Gifts Under $50

![Hero Image](/images/hero/lake-travis-yacht-sunset.webp)

You don't need to break the bank to delight the cocktail lover in your life. These thoughtful gifts under $50 show you understand their passion for hosting and mixing.

### Bar Tools & Accessories

Every home bartender needs quality tools. These essentials make great gifts:

- **Japanese-style jigger** ($12-20) - Precision measuring for perfect cocktails every time
- **Muddler** ($10-15) - Essential for mojitos, old fashioneds, and smashes
- **Cocktail picks** ($8-15) - Upgrade their garnish game with stainless steel or bamboo picks
- **Speed pourers** ($8-12 for a set) - Makes pouring smooth and consistent
- **Bar spoon** ($10-18) - The long twisted handle is designed for stirring and layering

### Austin Local Favorites

Support local Austin businesses while gifting something unique:

- **Yellowbird Hot Sauce gift set** - Perfect for Bloody Mary lovers
- **Austin Eastciders variety pack** - Local craft cider for the non-spirit drinker
- **Nixta Licor de Elote** - Award-winning Austin-made corn liqueur (great in margaritas)
- **LALO Blanco Tequila** - Premium Austin-connected tequila brand

### Books & Education

For the cocktail nerd who wants to learn:

- **"Death & Co" cocktail book** ($25-35) - The bible of modern cocktails
- **"Meehan's Bartender Manual"** ($30) - Comprehensive guide to technique
- **"Smuggler's Cove"** ($25) - For the tiki enthusiast

### Quality Mixers & Bitters

Elevate their cocktail game without breaking the bank:

- **Angostura bitters set** - The classic plus orange
- **Fee Brothers variety pack** - Explore unique flavors like black walnut and aztec chocolate
- **Fever-Tree mixers gift pack** - Premium tonic, ginger beer, and club soda
- **Q Mixers** - Spectacular ginger beer and tonic water

## The Sweet Spot: Gifts $50-$100

This is where you can really impress. Gifts in this range feel substantial and show you put thought into the selection.

### Cocktail Kits - The Perfect Gift

Pre-assembled cocktail kits are one of the best gifts for anyone who loves to entertain. They include everything needed to make a specific cocktail (except the spirit), making it easy to try something new.

**What makes a great cocktail kit:**
- Quality mixers and syrups
- Fresh garnishes or garnish ingredients
- Recipe card with technique tips
- Enough for multiple servings (great for hosting)

At Party On Delivery, we offer curated cocktail kits that pair perfectly with our alcohol delivery service. Gift the kit, and they can order the spirits to match - delivered right to their door.

**Popular cocktail kit themes:**
- **Margarita Kit** - Premium lime, agave, and Tajin rim
- **Old Fashioned Kit** - Bitters selection, demerara syrup, orange peel
- **Espresso Martini Kit** - Cold brew, vanilla, coffee liqueur mixer
- **Spicy Margarita Kit** - Jalapeño-infused elements and citrus

### Glassware Sets

Beautiful glassware elevates every drink. These make stunning gifts:

- **Coupe glasses (set of 4)** - $40-70 - For martinis, daiquiris, and champagne
- **Rocks glasses (set of 4)** - $35-60 - Essential for whiskey and old fashioneds
- **Highball glasses** - $40-65 - Perfect for gin & tonics, mojitos, ranch water
- **Nick & Nora glasses** - $50-80 - The elegant, vintage-inspired choice

### Local Austin Distillery Bottles

Support local while gifting something special:

- **Treaty Oak Ghost Hill Bourbon** (~$50) - Dripping Springs distillery, excellent for sipping
- **Desert Door Texas Sotol** (~$40) - Unique West Texas spirit, great conversation starter
- **Still Austin Bourbon** (~$45) - Grain-to-glass Austin whiskey
- **Tito's Handmade Vodka gift set** - The Austin original with branded glasses

### Experience Gifts

Sometimes the best gift is an experience:

- **Cocktail class gift certificate** - Several Austin bars offer classes ($50-75)
- **Distillery tour tickets** - Treaty Oak, Still Austin, or Desert Door
- **Wine tasting in Fredericksburg** - Hill Country day trip experience

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's a good gift for someone who loves making cocktails at home?

The best gifts for home bartenders include quality bar tools (jigger, shaker, bar spoon), cocktail kits with premium ingredients, cocktail recipe books, and unique bitters or mixers. For a bigger impact, consider a complete barware set or gift card to a premium alcohol delivery service so they can choose their own spirits.

### Can I get alcohol delivered as a gift in Austin?

Yes! Party On Delivery offers alcohol delivery throughout the Austin area. You can purchase a gift card for the recipient to use, or coordinate a delivery on their behalf. We require 72-hour advance notice for scheduled deliveries, with express 3-hour options available for last-minute needs. The recipient must be 21+ and present to receive the delivery.

### What are the best local Austin gifts for cocktail lovers?

Austin has an incredible local spirits scene. Great local options include Treaty Oak bourbon or gin from Dripping Springs, Desert Door Texas Sotol, Still Austin whiskey, Nixta corn liqueur, and of course Tito's Vodka. You can also gift experiences like distillery tours at these local producers.

### How much should I spend on a gift for someone who loves to entertain?

Gift budgets vary based on your relationship and occasion. For a thoughtful gesture, $25-50 covers quality bar tools, books, or mixers. For a more substantial gift, $75-150 gets you premium spirits, cocktail kits, or glassware sets. For a truly impressive gift, $200+ can cover a full home bar setup or party delivery package.

### What's a unique gift for someone who has everything?

For the person who has it all, consider experience gifts: private cocktail classes, distillery tours, Hill Country wine tastings, or a Lake Travis boat party. You can also gift a "stocked party" - we'll deliver everything they need for their next celebration, so they just have to show up and enjoy.
