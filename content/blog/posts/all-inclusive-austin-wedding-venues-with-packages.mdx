## All-Inclusive Austin Wedding Venues with Packages

Y'all, planning an Austin wedding can be equal parts exciting and overwhelming. With so many incredible venues to choose from, it can be tough to know where to start. But fear not, my fellow Texans! Party On Delivery is here to share our expert insights on the best all-inclusive wedding venues in the Live Music Capital of the World.

Whether you're dreaming of a grand ballroom affair, a rustic Hill Country soirée, or an urban-chic celebration, Austin has got you covered. We've scoped out the top spots that offer comprehensive packages to take the stress out of your big day. So grab a cold one, put on your dancing boots, and let's dive in!

## The Fairmont Austin: Luxury and Elegance Reimagined

![Hero Image](/images/hero/wedding-hero-garden.webp)


If you're after pure, unadulterated glamour, you can't go wrong with the [Fairmont Austin](https://www.fairmont.com/austin/). This AAA Four Diamond hotel is the epitome of sophisticated style, boasting over 37,000 square feet of event space that can accommodate weddings of up to 1,400 guests. 

Their all-inclusive wedding packages include an array of perks, like a champagne toast, custom wedding cake, and a dedicated wedding specialist to handle all the details. Plus, with their in-house catering and bar service, you can rest assured your guests will be wowed by the culinary experience. And let's not forget the stunning views of the Austin skyline - the perfect backdrop for your "I do's".

## Brazos Hall: Industrial Chic in the Heart of Downtown

For couples seeking a more modern, urban vibe, [Brazos Hall](https://brazoshall.com/) is a showstopper. This renovated warehouse-turned-event space boasts high ceilings, exposed brick, and tons of natural light - the perfect canvas for your wedding vision.

Their all-inclusive packages cover everything from tables and chairs to a full-service bar, making it a one-stop-shop for your celebration. And the best part? You're smack-dab in the heart of downtown Austin, so your guests can easily explore all the city has to offer before and after the big day.

## Barr Mansion: Organic Elegance in the Texas Countryside

If you're dreaming of a rustic-chic affair, the [Barr Mansion](https://barrmansion.com/) is a must-see. This certified organic events venue, located just 10 minutes from downtown Austin, offers a stunning 19th-century mansion and lush, landscaped grounds for your wedding day.

Their all-inclusive packages include everything from farm-to-table catering to on-site coordination and setup. And with their focus on sustainability, you can feel good knowing your celebration is eco-friendly. Plus, the mansion's elegant interiors and the property's natural beauty provide the perfect canvas for your personalized touches.

## The Oasis on Lake Travis: Panoramic Views and Waterfront Bliss

For the couple who wants to infuse their wedding with a little Hill Country charm, look no further than [The Oasis on Lake Travis](https://www.oasis-austin.com/). Perched atop a scenic bluff, this iconic venue boasts breathtaking views of the shimmering lake and rolling hills beyond.

Their all-inclusive wedding packages cover everything from tables and chairs to in-house catering and bar service. And with several ceremony and reception spaces to choose from, you can customize your day to perfectly suit your style. Whether you envision a lakeside ceremony or a twinkling reception under the stars, The Oasis has got you covered.

## Brix and Brass: Intimate Elegance in a Historic Setting

Sometimes, a more intimate setting is exactly what the heart desires. Enter [Brix and Brass](https://www.brixandbrass.com/), a charming event space housed in a 1930s-era building in the heart of downtown Austin.

With their all-inclusive wedding packages, you'll enjoy the perfect blend of historic charm and modern amenities. From the exposed brick walls to the stunning chandelier-lit ballroom, every inch of this venue exudes elegance. And with in-house catering and bar service, you can focus on creating unforgettable memories with your nearest and dearest.

## Practical Tips for Choosing Your Austin Wedding Venue

As you navigate the world of all-inclusive Austin wedding venues, here are a few insider tips to keep in mind:

- **Consider your guest count:** Make sure the venue can comfortably accommodate your vision, with enough space for dancing, dining, and mingling.
- **Think about accessibility:** Look for venues with ample parking, wheelchair-friendly access, and nearby accommodations for your out-of-town guests.
- **Prioritize your must-haves:** Whether it's a killer view, a bustling downtown location, or a focus on sustainability, identify your top priorities and let that guide your venue search.
- **Book early:** The best all-inclusive venues in Austin tend to fill up fast, so start your planning process as soon as possible.
- **Inquire about packages:** Don't be afraid to ask about any additional services or perks that might be included in the venue's all-inclusive offerings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What's the average cost of an all-inclusive wedding venue in Austin?
A: The cost of an all-inclusive wedding venue in Austin can vary widely, depending on factors like guest count, peak versus off-peak season, and the level of luxury or amenities included. On average, you can expect to pay between $10,000 to $30,000 for an all-inclusive package at a mid-to-high-end venue in the Austin area.

### Q: Do all-inclusive venues provide alcohol service?
A: Many all-inclusive wedding venues in Austin do offer in-house bar and alcohol service as part of their packages. This can include everything from a champagne toast to a fully stocked open bar, depending on the venue and package you choose. Be sure to ask about their alcohol policies and offerings when evaluating potential venues.

### Q: How far in advance should I book an all-inclusive Austin wedding venue?
A: It's generally recommended to book your all-inclusive Austin wedding venue at least 12-18 months in advance, especially if you're planning to tie the knot during the popular spring and fall seasons. Some of the most sought-after venues may require even earlier booking, so be sure to start your venue search as soon as you've set your wedding date.

### Q: Do all-inclusive venues handle the catering and rentals?
A: Yes, the vast majority of all-inclusive wedding venues in Austin will provide full-service catering and event rentals as part of their packages. This typically includes everything from tables and chairs to linens, china, and glassware. Some venues may even offer in-house floral design or other decorative elements to complete the look and feel of your wedding day.

### Q: Can I customize an all-inclusive wedding package?
A: Absolutely! While all-inclusive packages are designed to take the guesswork out of wedding planning, most venues are happy to work with you to customize the experience to fit your unique vision and budget. Don't be afraid to ask about any à la carte options or the ability to tailor the package to your specific needs.
