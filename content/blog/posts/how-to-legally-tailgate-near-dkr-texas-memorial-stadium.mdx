## How to Legally Tailgate Near DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium

It's game day in Austin, and the air is alive with the electric energy of Longhorns fans eager to cheer on their beloved team. As you make your way to [DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium](https://texassports.com/facilities/dkr-texas-memorial-stadium.html), the iconic home of the University of Texas Longhorns, the soundtrack of the day comes alive - the roar of the crowd, the clash of the bands, and the sizzle of burgers and brats on the grill.

Welcome to the world of Longhorns tailgating, a time-honored tradition that's as much a part of the game-day experience as the action on the field. Whether you're a seasoned tailgater or a newcomer to the scene, navigating the ins and outs of legal tailgating near DKR can be a bit of a challenge. But fear not, my fellow burnt orange-clad fans - we're here to share our insider tips and tricks to make your tailgating experience a touchdown-worthy celebration.

## Scope Out the Tailgating Zones

![Hero Image](/images/blog/how-to-legally-tailgate-near-dkr-texas-memorial-stadium/how-to-legally-tailgate-near-dkr-texas-memorial-stadium-0.webp)


The first step to successful tailgating near DKR is to understand the designated tailgating areas. The university has established specific zones where you're free to set up your party, each with its own unique vibe and amenities. 

The [San Jacinto Tailgating Zone](https://parking.utexas.edu/parking/events/football2025) is a popular spot, located just a stone's throw from the stadium. This expansive lot is perfect for larger groups and offers plenty of space to spread out and get the party started. Another option is the [Manor Lot](https://parking.utexas.edu/parking/events/football2025), which has a more intimate, community-like atmosphere and is great for smaller gatherings.

No matter which zone you choose, be sure to arrive early to secure the perfect spot. The lots typically open around 7 a.m. on game days, so plan accordingly and beat the rush.

## Familiarize Yourself with the Rules

Like any good party, there are a few rules to keep in mind when tailgating near DKR. The university has specific guidelines in place to ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience. 

For starters, all tailgating activities must take place within the designated zones. No wandering outside of the approved areas, as that could land you in hot water with the authorities. Additionally, the use of tents, canopies, and grills is permitted, but be mindful of the size and placement to avoid blocking walkways or infringing on your neighbors' space.

One important rule to keep in mind is the alcohol policy. While responsible drinking is allowed, the university has a zero-tolerance policy for public intoxication and disorderly conduct. Be sure to pace yourself and keep an eye on your fellow tailgaters to ensure everyone stays safe and has a good time.

## Navigate the Parking and Transportation Options

One of the biggest challenges of tailgating near DKR can be finding a place to park and navigating the crowds. Fortunately, the university has a comprehensive parking plan in place to make the process a little smoother.

If you're driving to the game, your best bet is to secure a parking pass in advance. The university offers a variety of options, from general lots to premium reserved spots. [Parking.utexas.edu](https://parking.utexas.edu/) is your one-stop-shop for all the details.

For those who prefer to leave the driving to someone else, consider booking a charter bus or shuttle service. [Longhorn Charter Bus](https://www.longhorncharterbus.com/) is a popular choice, offering reliable transportation to and from the stadium while you sit back and enjoy the ride.
