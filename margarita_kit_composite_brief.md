## Detailed Composition Guide

### Foreground (Sharp Focus)
- 1-2 margarita cocktails in rocks glasses
- Beautiful salt rim detail visible
- Fresh lime garnish (wheel or wedge)
- Casting subtle shadows on surface

### Midground (Main Focus)
- All 5 bottles arranged at varying heights
  - Tallest bottles (tequila, orange liqueur) toward back
  - Shorter bottles (lime juice, agave) toward front
  - Shaker positioned for visual interest
- Labels clearly visible but not overly prominent
- Products appear naturally arranged, not rigid

### Background (Soft/Atmospheric)
- Textured surface continues
- Additional limes or cocktail ingredients softly out of focus
- Warm, inviting atmosphere
- Depth of field creates professional editorial look

### Styling Details
- **Surface**: Rustic wood with visible grain OR textured cloth with pattern
- **Props**: Scattered fresh limes (whole and cut), perhaps bar tools
- **Glassware**: Quality rocks glasses, possibly with condensation for realism
- **Cocktail**: Beautiful golden-amber margarita color, clear ice
- **Salt Rim**: Detailed, appetizing presentation
