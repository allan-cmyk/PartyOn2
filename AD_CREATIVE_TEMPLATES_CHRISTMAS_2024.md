## 📹 Ad Template #5: Video/Reel Ad (15 seconds)

### Video Storyboard

**Duration:** 15 seconds
**Format:** 1080x1920 (vertical for Reels/Stories) OR 1080x1080 (square for Feed)
**Audio:** Upbeat holiday music (from Instagram music library)
**Voiceover:** None (text overlays only - 80% watch without sound)

---

### Shot Breakdown

**Shot 1 (0-3 seconds): Unboxing**
- **Visual:** Hands opening beautiful box, revealing wrapped contents
- **Text Overlay:** "Skip the liquor store"
- **Music:** Starts upbeat

**Shot 2 (4-7 seconds): Reveal Ingredients**
- **Visual:** Unpack items one by one - tequila, triple sec, lime juice
- **Text Overlay:** "Everything in one box"
- **Product Labels:** Clearly visible

**Shot 3 (8-11 seconds): Quick Mix Demo**
- **Visual:** Pour ingredients into pitcher, add ice, stir quickly
- **Text Overlay:** "Makes 24 margaritas"
- **Speed:** Slightly sped up (1.5x) for energy

**Shot 4 (12-15 seconds): Finished Product + CTA**
- **Visual:** Beautifully garnished margarita on table with festive background
- **Text Overlay:**
  - "$89.99"
  - "Order by Dec 20"
  - "Shop Now" button animation
- **Final Frame:** Logo + URL

---

### Video Script & Filming Notes

**Equipment Needed:**
- Smartphone (iPhone 13+ or equivalent)
- Good lighting (near window or ring light)
- Simple backdrop (white wall or neutral surface)
- Tripod or phone stand

**Filming Tips:**
1. **Shoot Vertical (9:16)** for Reels/Stories
2. **Natural Lighting** - Shoot during daytime near window
3. **Keep Hands in Frame** - Makes it feel authentic
4. **Focus on Product Labels** - Make sure brand names are visible
5. **Multiple Takes** - Film each shot 3-5 times, pick best
6. **Smooth Movements** - No jerky camera motion

**Editing (Use CapCut or Instagram):**
1. Trim clips to 3-4 seconds each
2. Add text overlays (bold, white text with black outline)
3. Add upbeat music (Instagram Reels music library)
4. Add "Shop Now" button animation at end
5. Export in 1080x1920 for Stories/Reels

## 📊 Ad Performance Testing Plan

### What to Test (A/B Testing)

**Week 1-2: Test Creative Formats**
- Test 1: Static Hero Shot vs. Carousel
- Test 2: Lifestyle vs. Product-Only Image
- Test 3: Video vs. Static Images

**Week 2-3: Test Copy Angles**
- Angle 1: Convenience ("Skip the store")
- Angle 2: Value ("24 drinks for $89")
- Angle 3: Gifting ("Perfect for hosts")

**Week 3-4: Test CTAs**
- CTA 1: "Shop Now"
- CTA 2: "Learn More"
- CTA 3: "Order Gift Kit"

### Success Metrics
- **CTR > 1.5%** = Winner (keep running)
- **CTR 1-1.5%** = Average (test variations)
- **CTR < 1%** = Pause and replace

## 🚀 Uploading to Meta Ads Manager

**5. Review & Publish**

- [ ] Clean all product bottles (fingerprints off)

## 💡 Pro Tips for High-Performing Ad Creative

### What Works on Meta Ads

**DO:**
✅ Show the product clearly (no confusion about what you're selling)
✅ Use bright, high-quality images (Instagram loves vibrant visuals)
✅ Add text overlays with key info (price, value prop)
✅ Include faces/hands when possible (increases engagement)
✅ Test video (video ads typically outperform static by 20-30%)
✅ Keep copy concise (mobile users scroll fast)
✅ Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per ad, not excessive)
✅ Create urgency (December 20 deadline)

**DON'T:**
❌ Use low-resolution images (blurry, pixelated)
❌ Overcrowd with text (Meta penalizes text-heavy images)
❌ Use stock photos that look generic
❌ Forget to show the actual product
❌ Use all caps in copy (looks spammy)
❌ Make claims you can't deliver ("Best gift ever!")
