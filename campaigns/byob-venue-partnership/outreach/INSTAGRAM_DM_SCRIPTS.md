# Instagram DM Scripts - BYOB Venue Partnership Campaign

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Purpose:** Warm outreach to venues via Instagram direct messages

---

## Overview

Instagram DMs work best as a **warm channel** - use them after engaging with the venue's content first. These scripts are designed to feel personal and conversational, not salesy.

**Before DMing:**
1. Follow the venue account
2. Like 2-3 recent posts
3. Leave a genuine comment on a recent post
4. Wait 1-2 days, then DM

---

## Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday - Thursday
- **Best times:** 10am-2pm or 6pm-9pm (when they're checking social media)
- **Avoid:** Weekends (they're busy with events)

### Engagement Before Outreach
- Follow first, wait 24 hours
- Like at least 3 posts before DMing
- Comment genuinely on 1-2 posts
- Never DM the same day you follow

### Tone Guidelines
- Conversational, not corporate
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max per message)
- Keep messages under 150 words
- Ask questions to encourage response

### What NOT to Do
- Don't send the same message to multiple venues (they may compare)
- Don't follow up more than 3 times total
- Don't be pushy or salesy
- Don't mention competitors by name
- Don't spam their comments
