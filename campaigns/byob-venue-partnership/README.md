# BYOB Venue Partnership Campaign

**Campaign Status:** 🟡 In Development
**Target Launch:** 2-3 weeks
**Owner:** PartyOn Delivery
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Campaign Overview

A multi-channel outreach campaign to establish partnerships with BYOB venues across Austin and surrounding Hill Country areas. Goal is to become the preferred alcohol delivery vendor for these venues.

## Campaign Objectives

1. **Primary:** Sign 15-20 new venue partnerships within 60 days of launch
2. **Secondary:** Drive traffic to BYOB venues page (proof for future outreach)
3. **Tertiary:** Increase wedding/event orders through venue referrals

## Target Market

- **Total Venues Identified:** 73
- **Geographic Scope:** Austin, Travis County, and nearby Hill Country (Dripping Springs, Georgetown, Kyle, Buda, Lockhart)
- **Priority Segments:**
  - Wedding venues (barns, ranches, estates) - Highest value
  - Party boats/lake experiences - Unique niche
  - Industrial/modern event spaces - Corporate events
  - Art studios/entertainment - Volume play

## Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Commission | Benefits | Requirements |
|------|------------|----------|--------------|
| **Premier Partner** | 7% | Video feature, #1 placement, free delivery badge, co-marketing | Exclusive preferred vendor, prominent link, display materials |
| **Featured Partner** | 5% | Badge, featured section, priority listing | Preferred vendor mention, website link |
| **Listed Partner** | 3% | Basic listing on BYOB page | Link to PartyOn from website |
