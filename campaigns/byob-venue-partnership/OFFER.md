## The Opportunity

PartyOn Delivery is Austin's premier alcohol delivery service for events and special occasions. We're building a network of preferred BYOB venue partners to create a seamless experience for couples and event planners.

**Why Partner With Us?**
- We deliver directly to your venue (customers don't transport alcohol)
- We calculate quantities for them (no over-buying or running out)
- We handle returns on unopened product (no waste for customers)
- We're fully licensed, insured, and TABC compliant (protects you)
- We drive traffic to your venue through our marketing

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do we have to be exclusive with PartyOn?**
A: Only Premier Partners commit to exclusive preferred status. Featured and Listed partners can recommend multiple options.

**Q: What if a customer wants to buy their own alcohol elsewhere?**
A: That's completely fine. We're here as a convenient option, not a requirement. You just mention us as an option.

**Q: How do we get paid?**
A: Commission is calculated monthly and paid via venmo or paypal by the 15th of the following month.

**Q: What if there's a delivery issue?**
A: We handle all customer service. If anything goes wrong, we fix it - you're never responsible for delivery issues.

**Q: Can we upgrade tiers later?**
A: Absolutely. Many partners start as Listed and upgrade to Featured or Premier as they see results.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**
A: No long-term contracts. Either party can end the partnership with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business.

**Q: What about liability/insurance?**
A: PartyOn carries full liability insurance and maintains all required TABC licenses. Your venue is protected.
